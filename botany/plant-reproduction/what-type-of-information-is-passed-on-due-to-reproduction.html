PLANT REPRODUCTION

[SOURCES]
What type of information is passed on due to reproduction?

(A) Plants

(B) Specialized Structures

(C) ** Genetic

(D) Offspring

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Genetic information is passed from generation to generation through inherited units of chemical information (in most cases, genes). Organisms produce other similar organisms through sexual reproduction, which allows the line of genetic material to be maintained and generations to be linked.

Concept note-2: -DNA is inherited from each parent by the process of reproduction. Reproduction is the process of the formation of a new individual to pass down the genetics by the process of fusion of the male and female gamete.

Concept note-3: -The genetic information is transferred from the nucleus to the cytoplasm by RNA (Ribonucleic acid). RNA was discovered by Severo Ochoa. In eukaryotic cells, pre-mRNA has been transcribed from DNA (Deoxyribonucleic acid) and processed to mature mRNA.

Concept note-4: -We now know that the DNA carries the hereditary information of the cell (Figure 4-2). In contrast, the protein components of chromosomes function largely to package and control the enormously long DNA molecules so that they fit inside cells and can easily be accessed by them.

Concept note-5: -Genetic information is carried in the linear sequence of nucleotides in DNA. Each molecule of DNA is a double helix formed from two complementary strands of nucleotides held together by hydrogen bonds between G-C and A-T base pairs.
