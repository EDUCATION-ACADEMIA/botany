An advantage of tissue culture is that only one plant can be made from a disease-free plant.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An advantage of tissue culture is that only one plant can be made from a disease free plant. Double eye cuttings are often used when plants have opposite leaves. Herbaceous plants are propagated by cuttings.

Concept note-2: -Following are the various advantages of tissue culture technique: The plantlets are obtained in a very short time with a small amount of plant tissue. The new plants produced are disease-free. The plants can be grown throughout the year, irrespective of the season.

Concept note-3: -Meristems are virus-free, and they are cultured on a suitable medium of shoot apical meristems to obtain virus-free plants. Q. Assertion :Through tissue culture exclusively disease resistant plant can be obtained. Reason: Plants develop from meristem are disease free and resistant.

Concept note-4: -Explanation: Plant tissue culture is the technique of in vitro maintain and growing cells, tissues, organs, etc.; and their differentiation on artificial medium under aseptic conditions inside suitable containers.

Concept note-5: -Totipotency is the ability of plant cells, where a cell can change to a meristematic state and can be differentiated into a whole plant. This property of plant cells has an advantage over Animal cell tissue culture.
