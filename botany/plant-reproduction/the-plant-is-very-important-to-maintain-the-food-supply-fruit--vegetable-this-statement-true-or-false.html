The plant is very important to maintain the food supply (fruit & vegetable). This statement true or false?

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The given statement is false. The leaves, roots, stems, fruits and seeds of various plants can all act as sources of food.

Concept note-2: -FALSE. Explanation: WE GET FOOD FROM ANIMALS AS WELL.

Concept note-3: -All plants are not edible for us. Some plants are poisonous and harmful to us. Some plant products cannot be digested by our alimentary canal. Only a few plants and their products are edible for us.

Concept note-4: -The basic food for all organisms is produced by green plants. In the process of food production, oxygen is released. This oxygen, which we obtain from the air we breathe, is essential to life. The only source of food and oxygen are plants; no animal alone can supply these.
