Plant is very important to living things because its will supply oxygen and food. This statement true or false?

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Plants, like animals, take in oxygen and give out carbon dioxide during respiration. Only during photosynthesis, plants take in carbon dioxide and release oxygen as a byproduct.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Solution: False; green plants prepare their food by using raw materials, carbon dioxide, chlorophyll, water and light.

Concept note-3: -The correct option is A True. During photosynthesis, Carbon dioxide(CO2) gas is taken in through stomata from the atmosphere. Oxygen(O2) gas is released through stomata into the atmosphere as a by-product.

Concept note-4: -Plants provide us with food, fiber, shelter, medicine, and fuel. The basic food for all organisms is produced by green plants. In the process of food production, oxygen is released. This oxygen, which we obtain from the air we breathe, is essential to life.
