Engineered cells of some plants like petunias, tobacco, and tomatoes can readily be used to regenerate entire plants under sterile conditions. For this to take place the the cells must return to an ____ state.

(A) asexual

(B) ** undifferentiated

(C) dormate

(D) cellular

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Role of Stem Cells in Plant Organ Regeneration The first case refers to the possibility of regenerating plant organs by culturing explants and inducing callus, a tissue for a long time considered to be a quite disorganized mass of undifferentiated cells [20].

Concept note-2: -Specifically, plant tissue culture is a collection of techniques used to maintain or grow plant cells, tissues or organs under sterile conditions on a nutrient culture medium of known composition. Plant tissue culture is widely used to produce clones of a plant in a method known as micropropagation.

Concept note-3: -Unlike xylem conducting cells, phloem conducting cells are alive at maturity. The xylem and phloem always lie adjacent to each other (Figure 3).

Concept note-4: -Any viable plant cell can differentiate into somatic embryos.
