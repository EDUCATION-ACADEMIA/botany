PLANT REPRODUCTION

[SOURCES]
Reproduction means to

(A) ** Make new life (plant or animals)

(B) Death

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reproduction (or procreation or breeding) is the biological process by which new individual organisms – “offspring” – are produced from their “parent” or parents. Reproduction is a fundamental feature of all known life; each individual organism exists as the result of reproduction.

Concept note-2: -Reproduction is the biological process by which organisms give birth or give rise to a new organism. This process is seen in all living organisms-both plants and animals.

Concept note-3: -Reproduction is the production of offspring. There are two main forms: sexual and asexual reproduction. In sexual reproduction, an organism combines the genetic information from each of its parents and is genetically unique. In asexual reproduction, one parent copies itself to form a genetically identical offspring.

Concept note-4: -Reproduction is a biological process by which an organism reproduces an offspring that is biologically similar to the organism. Reproduction enables and ensures the continuity of species, generation after generation. It is the main feature of life on earth.

Concept note-5: -All organisms reproduce, which is the biological process where an organism produces and/or gives birth to another organism. Both plants and animals reproduce, though they have evolved the processes so that they overlap and diverge from each other in several ways.
