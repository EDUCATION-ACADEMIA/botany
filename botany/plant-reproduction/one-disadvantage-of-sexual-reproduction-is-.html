One disadvantage of sexual reproduction is ____

(A) ** more energy needed

(B) less energy needed

(C) clone of parents

(D) variation of offspring

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The disadvantages of sexual reproduction: time and energy are needed to find a mate. it is not possible for an isolated individual to reproduce.

Concept note-2: -Problem 5: Asexual vs. sexual reproduction When conditions become more stressful reproduction switches to a sexual mode. Why? C. Asexual reproduction requires more energy.

Concept note-3: -Reproduction is one of the most costly activities in terms of energy expenditure in female mammals.

Concept note-4: -Sexual reproduction can only take place when appropriate male and female mates meet and the process of fertilization occurs. However, asexual reproduction requires less energy investment as only one parent organism is required to form a new daughter organism.
