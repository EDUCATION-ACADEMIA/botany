Which of the following is an advantage of using asexual reproduction methods to grow new plants?

(A) Faster time to a mature plant

(B) Create large numbers of identical plants

(C) Keep best traits pure

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An additional advantage of asexual reproduction is that colonization of new habitats may be easier when an individual does not need to find a mate to reproduce. During sexual reproduction the genetic material of two individuals is combined to produce genetically diverse offspring that differ from their parents.

Concept note-2: -Asexual reproduction is especially suitable to plants as they are non-motile. There are several advantages of asexual reproduction including the maintenance of genetic continuity, decreased energy requirements, numerous offspring produced quickly, and that fact that it does not require finding a mate.

Concept note-3: -The advantages of asexual reproduction include: the population can increase rapidly when the conditions are favourable. only one parent is needed. it is more time and energy efficient as you donâ€™t need a mate.

Concept note-4: -Asexual reproduction has its advantages and disadvantages. In some cases, identical clones produced through asexual reproduction have the best traits for survival. In other situations, producing clones leaves a plant species vulnerable to population decline or extinction.
