Has membrane bound organelles

(A) Prokaryotes

(B) ** Eukaryote

(C) both

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eukaryotic cells like plant cell and animal cells contain a well defined nucleus and have all the membrane bound organelles. E.g., Golgi bodies, mitochondria, endoplasmic reticulum, etc. Whereas prokaryotic cells like bacterial cells do not contain membrane bound cell organelles.

Concept note-2: -The cells that have membrane bound organelles are called eukaryotic.

Concept note-3: -Eukaryotic cells contain several membrane-bound structures, or organelles, which are specialized cellular subunits that carry out specific cellular functions. The nucleus is surrounded by the nuclear membrane, also called the nuclear envelope, which protects the genetic material stored inside.
