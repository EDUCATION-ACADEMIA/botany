What Kingdom do I belong to?I am multicelled.My cells do have a nucleus.I love the sun! It helps me make my food through photosynthesis.I can be found anywhere in the world.

(A) Archaeabacteria

(B) Protista

(C) ** Platae

(D) Fungi

(E) Animalia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kingdom Plantae and Animalia include multicellular organisms. Animals have distinct organ systems such as the digestive system, respiratory system, etc.

Concept note-2: -Animalia. Animals are multicellular, and move with the aid of cilia, flagella, or muscular organs based on contractile proteins. They have organelles including a nucleus, but no chloroplasts or cell walls. Animals acquire nutrients by ingestion.

Concept note-3: -Algae, along with plants and some bacteria and fungi, are autotrophs. Autotrophs are the producers in the food chain, meaning they create their own nutrients and energy. Kelp, like most autotrophs, creates energy through a process called photosynthesis.

Concept note-4: -All multicellular eukaryotic organisms are classified as members of the kingdom Plantae. They are autotrophs because they use solar energy to produce their food.
