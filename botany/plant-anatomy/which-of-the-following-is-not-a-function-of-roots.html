Which of the following is NOT a function of roots?

(A) Store food

(B) Absorb water

(C) Anchor the plant

(D) ** Make food

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -∴ Starch synthesis is not a function of root.

Concept note-2: -Roots do not absorb sunlight as they are below the ground. Sunlight is absorbed by green parts of the plant like the leaves. Q.

Concept note-3: -Roots help in absorption of water and minerals from the soil, which are then transported to other parts of the plant.

Concept note-4: -Explanation: A vascular plant’s, root is the underground portion of the plant. Its key tasks include plant anchoring, water and dissolved mineral absorption and transmission to the stem and reserve food storage.
