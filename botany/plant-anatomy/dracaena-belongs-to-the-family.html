Dracaena belongs to the family

(A) ** Asparagaceae

(B) Apiaceae

(C) Liliaceae

(D) Bignoniaceae

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dracaena, genus of about 170 species of flowering plants in the asparagus family (Asparagaceae). Members of the genus are native primarily to the Old World tropics, especially Africa, and one species is endemic to South America.

Concept note-2: -Native to hot and humid climates such as Asia, Central America and parts of tropical Africa, Dracaena, is a genus of about 120 species flowering evergreen trees shrubs in the asparagus family.

Concept note-3: -Other English names include striped dracaena (for variegated cultivars), corn plant (for the cultivar ‘Massangeana’; ), Chinese money tree, and fortune plant.
