What type of adaptation is dormancy?

(A) Physical Adaptation

(B) ** Behavioral Adaptation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Animals and birds usually practise this type of adaptation to survive in their environments. Dormancy, camouflage, hibernation and migration are different types of behavioural adaptations practised by both plants and animals.

Concept note-2: -Behavioral adaptations allow animals to respond to life needs. Examples include hibernation, migration, dormancy, instinct, and learned behavior.

Concept note-3: -Behavioral adaptation: something an animal does usually in response to some type of external stimulus in order to survive. Hibernating during winter is an example of a behavioral adaptation.

Concept note-4: -Adaptations may help organisms to survive and sometimes to reproduce. There are three different categories into which we can class different sorts of adaptations: behavioral, structural, and functional adaptations. Bird songs are an example of a behavioral adaptation specifically.

Concept note-5: -Examples of behavioral adaptation include migration, hibernation, learned behavior, alteration in the mode of reproduction, altered feeding habits, and distinct modes of communication.
