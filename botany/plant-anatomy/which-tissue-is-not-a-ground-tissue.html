Which tissue is not a ground tissue?

(A) perenchyma tissue

(B) collenchyma tissue

(C) ** epidermal tissue

(D) sclerenchyma tissue

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ground tissue is all plant tissues except vascular and dermal tissues.

Concept note-2: -Final answer: Ground tissue includes all tissues except epidermis and vascular bundles.

Concept note-3: -So, the correct answer is ‘Companion cells.

Concept note-4: -In some works, the cells of the leaf epidermis are regarded as specialised parenchymal cells, but the modern preference has long been to classify the epidermis as plant dermal tissue, and parenchyma as ground tissue.

Concept note-5: -It differentiates into three main types of tissues – dermal, vascular, and ground tissue.
