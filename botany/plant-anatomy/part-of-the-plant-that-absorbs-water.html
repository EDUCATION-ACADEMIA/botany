Part of the plant that absorbs water.

(A) ** Roots

(B) Flowers

(C) Petals

(D) Leaves

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: The plants absorb water from the soil with the help of root hair through osmosis. The diffusion of water molecules through a semi-permeable membrane is called osmosis. Root hair absorbs water from soil through osmosis.

Concept note-2: -Plants absorb water through their entire surface – roots, stems and leaves. However, the majority of water is absorbed by root hairs. Root hairs are thin-walled uni-cellular outgrowths of epidermis.

Concept note-3: -Roots of the plant carry out the function of absorbing water and minerals from the soil.
