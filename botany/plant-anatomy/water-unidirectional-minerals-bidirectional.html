EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The transport of water in the xylem takes place only from the roots to the leaves. Therefore, the movement of water and nutrients in the xylem is unidirectional.

Concept note-2: -Organic and mineral nutrients undergo multidirectional transport from leaves to different parts of the plant.

Concept note-3: -So, the correct answer is ‘Xylem’.

Concept note-4: -Phloem transport is bidirectional because sucrose gets accumulated in the phloem tissue and absorbs water creating a high turgor pressure. Other areas have low turgor pressure due to loss of water.
