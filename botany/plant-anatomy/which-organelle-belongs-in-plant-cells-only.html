Which organelle belongs in plant cells only?

(A) Cytoplasm

(B) Mitochondrion

(C) ** Cell Wall

(D) Nucleus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chloroplasts are the food producers of the cell. The organelles are only found in plant cells and some protists such as algae. Animal cells do not have chloroplasts. Chloroplasts work to convert light energy of the Sun into sugars that can be used by cells.

Concept note-2: -Plant cell needs cell wall whereas animal cell do not because the plants need rigid structure so that they can grow up and out . All cells have cell membranes, and the membranes are flexible. So animal cells can have various shapes, but plant cells only have the shapes of their cell walls.

Concept note-3: -The organelles found only in plant cells include-chloroplast, cell wall, plastids, and a large central vacuole.
