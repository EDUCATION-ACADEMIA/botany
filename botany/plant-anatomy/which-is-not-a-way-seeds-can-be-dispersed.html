Which is NOT a way seeds can be dispersed?

(A) animals

(B) wind

(C) water

(D) ** photosynthesis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The modes of dispersion of seeds are gravity, wind, water, explosion and animals. Sunlight doesn’t have any direct effect on dispersion of seeds.

Concept note-2: -Because plants cannot walk around and take their seeds to other places, they have developed other methods to disperse (move) their seeds. The most common methods are wind, water, animals, explosion and fire.

Concept note-3: -The methods of seed dispersal are: Gravity. Wind. Ballistic. Water.

Concept note-4: -Wind, water, animals, explosion and human are considered generally as agents of seed dispersal. Whereas, sunlight doesn’t have any direct effect on the dispersion of seeds.

Concept note-5: -Since the seed is in a dormant stage, chloroplasts are not present in seeds and therefore cannot synthesize the materials needed for photosynthesis to occur. Therefore, there is a need for a stored nutrition compartment in seeds which is the endosperm.
