Which is not a function of stems?

(A) Hold up the leaves

(B) Transport water and nutrients to different parts of the plant

(C) ** Absorb water from soil

(D) All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Photosynthesis is not a primary function of stem. The process by which leaves prepare their food in the presence of sunlight by using water and carbon dioxide is called photosynthesis.

Concept note-2: -(i) It supports branches, leaves, flowers, and fruits. (ii) It transports water and minerals from the roots to the leaves and other parts of plants. (iii) It transports food from leaves to different parts of the plant. (iv) It holds the plant upright.

Concept note-3: -1. Stem absorbs water and minerals from the soil. 2. Leaves hold the plant upright.

Concept note-4: -So, The correct answer is option (b) Photosynthesis.
