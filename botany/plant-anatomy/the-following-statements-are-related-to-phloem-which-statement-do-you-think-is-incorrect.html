The following statements are related to Phloem. Which statement do you think is incorrect?

(A) Phloem usually transports food from leaves to different parts of the plant

(B) Phloem is composed of Sieve tube, Companion cells, Phloem parenchyme and Phloem fibres

(C) Gymnosperms do not have Sieve tube and companion cells

(D) ** Phloem parenchyma is absent in most of the dicots

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We can find these phloem parenchyma in dicot leaf, dicot stem and monocot root but these are absent in monocot stem.

Concept note-2: -Complete answer: These are living and elongated parenchymatous cells that store food, resins, latex, etc. It has abundant plasmodesmata for the lateral conduction of food. Phloem parenchyma is absent in most of the monocots. Thus, the monocot stem is the one among the options that do not contain phloem parenchyma.

Concept note-3: -Phloem Parenchyma is absent in monocot stem.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Phloem transports photosynthetic products. Phloem is a living tissue in vascular plants that conducts the soluble organic compounds synthesized downwards from the leaves during photosynthesis.
