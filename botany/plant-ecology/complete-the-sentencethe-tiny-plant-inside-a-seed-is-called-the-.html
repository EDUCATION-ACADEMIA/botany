Complete the sentence.The tiny plant inside a seed is called the ____

(A) young adult

(B) seed coat

(C) ** embryo

(D) baby plant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final answer: A baby plant called an embryo is inside the seed coat.

Concept note-2: -"The embryo is the tiny plant protected by the seed coat.” watered.” “The cotyledon is the food for the embryo."

Concept note-3: -The mature embryo consists of an embryonic root known as the radicle, an embryonic shoot, and one or two cotyledons. The embryonic shoot, known as the plumule, has two main parts, the epicotyl and the hypocotyl.

Concept note-4: -The germ is the plant embryo which would form the new plant if germinated and is characterized by a high lipid and protein content.
