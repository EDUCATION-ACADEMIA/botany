Community of different species interacting with one another and with their nonliving environment of matter and energy.

(A) Population

(B) Community

(C) Biome

(D) ** Ecosystem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ecosystem: The collection of all living organisms in a geographic area, together with all the living and non-living things with which they interact.

Concept note-2: -An ecosystem consists of a community of organisms together with their physical environment. Ecosystems can be of different sizes and can be marine, aquatic, or terrestrial. Broad categories of terrestrial ecosystems are called biomes. In ecosystems, both matter and energy are conserved.

Concept note-3: -An ecosystem is a community of organisms and their physical environment interacting together. For food, shelter, growth and development, all life systems interact with the environment.

Concept note-4: -An ecosystem is the community of organisms that live in a particular area and along with their nonliving surroundings.

Concept note-5: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is b. ecosystem.
