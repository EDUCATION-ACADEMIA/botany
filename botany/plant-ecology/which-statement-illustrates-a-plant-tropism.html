Which statement illustrates a plant tropism?

(A) ** stem bends toward light.

(B) an apple develops from a flower.

(C) water moves through vascular tissue

(D) carbon dioxide diffuses out of a stem.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Expert-Verified Answer. A stem is a plant part that has a negative tropism towards the gravity stimulus. Positive tropism is growth towards a stimulus. For example, a plant moves towards sunlight.

Concept note-2: -One important light response in plants is phototropism, which involves growth toward-or away from-a light source.

Concept note-3: -Phototropism: The bending of growing plants towards the light is called phototropism. By orienting their leaves and stems towards light plants receive the maximum energy for photosynthesis.

Concept note-4: -Auxin hormone controls the plantâ€™s response towards light and makes the shoot region of the plant bend towards the light.

Concept note-5: -When light falls on one side of the plant, the auxins generally diffuse towards the shaded side of the shoot. This stimulates the cells in the shaded area to grow longer than the corresponding cells of the illuminated region. This results in the curvature of the plant stem tip towards the light.
