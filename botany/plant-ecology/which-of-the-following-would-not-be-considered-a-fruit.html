Which of the following would NOT be considered a fruit?

(A) Strawberries

(B) Blueberries

(C) Tomatoes

(D) Cotton

(E) ** Potatoes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -No worries! We’ve got your back. Try BYJU’S free classes today!

Concept note-2: -A fruit is defined as a part of a plant that develops from the ovary of a flower and contains seeds. Vegetables develop from other plant parts, such as roots, leaves, and stems. Potatoes are thickened stem and hence not a fruit.

Concept note-3: -Potatoes are tubers, which are a sort of root vegetable. They aren’t the potato plant’s roots themselves, but rather the roots that they grow from. Potatoes are absolutely edible, and they’re virtually always used in savoury meals, whether with or without meat. Potatoes, on the other hand, are not fruits.

Concept note-5: -Potato flowers and fruit are produced because potatoes multiply by seed. The edible part of the potato plant is a tuber connected to the roots of the plant.
