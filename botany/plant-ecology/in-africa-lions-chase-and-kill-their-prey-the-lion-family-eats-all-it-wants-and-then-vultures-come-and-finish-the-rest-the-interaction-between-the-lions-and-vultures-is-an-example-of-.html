In Africa, lions chase and kill their prey. The lion family eats all it wants and then vultures come and finish the rest. The interaction between the lions and vultures is an example of-

(A) ** the lions not being harmed by the relationship and the vultures benefiting

(B) the lions benefiting from the relationship and the vultures being harmed

(C) the lions benefiting by consuming the vultures as food

(D) both the lions and the vultures competing with one another for food

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Energy decreases as it moves up trophic levels because energy is lost as metabolic heat when the organisms from one trophic level are consumed by organisms from the next level.

Concept note-2: -Animals breathe oxygen and exhale carbon dioxide. Plants take in carbon dioxide and release oxygen back into the air. 2. Animals need plants for food and shelter.

Concept note-3: -In a food chain, the sun provides energy for an apple tree to grow. A deer eats an apple from the tree.
