Order the words in the most logical way according to the events they take part in

(A) DNA, protein, translation, transcription

(B) ** DNA, transcription, translation, protein

(C) DNA, translation, transcription, protein

(D) DNA, transcription, protein, translation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During translation, the genetic code in mRNA is read and used to make a polypeptide. These two processes are summed up by the central dogma of molecular biology: DNA → RNA → Protein .

Concept note-2: -The official order of protein synthesis is DNA to RNA to protein. DNA is transcribed into mRNA by a certain RNA polymerase holoenzyme complex. The mRNA is then transported from the nucleus and delivered to nearby ribosomes, where it is translated into proteins by tRNAs and other enzymes.

Concept note-3: -The major steps of transcription are initiation, promoter clearance, elongation, and termination.
