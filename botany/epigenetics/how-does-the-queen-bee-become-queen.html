How does the Queen bee become queen.

(A) She marries Jay-Z.

(B) ** Eating royal jelly silences a gene that would keep her a worker bee.

(C) Eating royal jelly activates the DMNT gene.

(D) She consume the worker bees, thus activating the queen bee gene.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A female larva destined to become a queen is fed large quantities of royal jelly; this triggers a cascade of molecular events resulting in development of a queen. It has been shown that this phenomenon is mediated by an epigenetic modification of DNA known as CpG methylation.

Concept note-2: -An egg which is required to develop into a Queen is fed a diet of Royal Jelly, nothing else. This causes that egg to develop faster, and bigger-with the full reproductive capabilities that only the Queen honey bee has.

Concept note-3: -Summary. A new study shows that, in honey bees, the main role of certain proteins in royal jelly is to ensure that the larva stays in its cell, thereby allowing it to develop into a queen.

Concept note-4: -A developing queen bee is fed royal jelly exclusively-not pollen and honey like her proletarian sisters. Some describe withholding royal jelly from worker bees as nutritional castration. These bees donâ€™t get the special Food of the Gods. Or, perhaps, food of genetic monarchies.

Concept note-5: -Intriguingly, one of the components of royal jelly is phenyl butyrate, a known histone deacetylase (HDAC) inhibitor [13]. Histone deacetylases catalyse the removal of acetyl groups from histones, which may allow for chromatin to become more compacted, so repressing transcription [14].
