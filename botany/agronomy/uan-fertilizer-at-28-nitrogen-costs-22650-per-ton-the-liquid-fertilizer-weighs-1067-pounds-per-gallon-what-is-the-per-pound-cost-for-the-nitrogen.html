(A) $0.37/pound

(B) ** $0.40/pound

(C) $0.55/pound

(D) $0.63/pound

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thirty-two percent UAN (32-0-0) weighs 11.06 pounds per gallon and salts out (solids separate from the liquid) at 32째F. Twenty-eight percent (28-0-0-5) contains 5 percent sulfur in addition to the nitrogen.
