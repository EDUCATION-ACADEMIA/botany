Concept note-1: -What Is Optimization Engineering? The job duties of an optimization engineer focus on taking an existing design and improving making it stronger, fast, more efficient, or more durable.

Concept note-2: -Process optimization is the discipline of adjusting a process so as to optimize (make the best or most effective use of) some specified set of parameters without violating some constraint. The most common goals are minimizing cost and maximizing throughput and/or efficiency.
