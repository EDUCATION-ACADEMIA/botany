Mention the different sources of potable water

(A) ground water

(B) snow pack water

(C) spring water

(D) ** all of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Potable water is the drinkable water available on Earth. The main source of potable water can be found in the surface water, collected rainwater, and groundwater.

Concept note-2: -Potable water, also known as drinking water, comes from surface and ground sources and is treated to levels that that meet state and federal standards for consumption. Water from natural sources is treated for microorganisms, bacteria, toxic chemicals, viruses and fecal matter.

Concept note-3: -Purified water is usually tap or groundwater which has been treated to remove harmful substances like bacteria, fungi, and parasites. This means that drinking it is pretty much guaranteed to be safe.
