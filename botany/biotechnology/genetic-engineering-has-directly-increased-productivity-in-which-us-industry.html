(A) ** agriculture

(B) automobile

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Genetic modification of plants involves adding a specific stretch of DNA into the plantâ€™s genome, giving it new or different characteristics. This could include changing the way the plant grows, or making it resistant to a particular disease.

Concept note-2: -The possible benefits of genetic engineering include: More nutritious food. Tastier food. Disease-and drought-resistant plants that require fewer environmental resources (such as water and fertilizer)

Concept note-3: -Biotechnology genetically modifies crops to make them toxic against insect attacks making them pest-resistant crops. The GMOs (genetically modified organisms) like transgenic crops are created through biotechnology to become more disease-resistant and enhance nutritional value for increasing crop yield.

Concept note-4: -Many GMO crops are used to make ingredients that Americans eat such as cornstarch, corn syrup, corn oil, soybean oil, canola oil, or granulated sugar. A few fresh fruit and vegetables are available in GMO varieties, including potatoes, summer squash, apples, papayas, and pink pineapples.
