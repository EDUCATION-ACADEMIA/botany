John recently earned his doctoral degree, which job is he MOST LIKELY pursuing?

(A) ** Research scientist

(B) Sales representative

(C) Technical writer

(D) Veterinary technician

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Carrying out research towards a doctoral degree. Participating in theoretical and/or empirical research in the relevant areas. Publishing results in the appropriate media. Presenting findings at conferences and seminars.

Concept note-2: -They can work in various departments of the government, such as policy making, rural development, transportation, scientific research, military, international relations, etc. One can procure various PhD jobs in India in the government sector.

Concept note-3: -In general, the most natural job choice after a PhD is becoming a University professor, Industrial R&D Lab professionals and Start-ups mentor. Academia has always been a favourite choice after completing the PhD studies. The job profile under this is similar to what the person did in their PhD.
