Which best describes biotechnology?

(A) a field of science that has not impacted our lives

(B) technological application of living and nonliving organisms to produce beneficial products

(C) a slowly growing field of science

(D) ** technological application of living organisms to produce beneficial products

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biotechnology is the branch of applied science that uses living organisms and their derivatives to produce products and processes. These products and processes feature in healthcare, medicine, biofuels, and environmental safety.

Concept note-2: -Biotechnology is technology that utilizes biological systems, living organisms or parts of this to develop or create different products. Brewing and baking bread are examples of processes that fall within the concept of biotechnology (use of yeast (= living organism) to produce the desired product).

Concept note-3: -Specifically, industrial biotechnology uses enzymes and micro-organisms to make bio-based products in sectors such as chemicals, food ingredients, detergents, paper, textiles and biofuels.

Concept note-4: -Biotechnology is the use of biology to develop new products, methods and organisms intended to improve human health and society.
