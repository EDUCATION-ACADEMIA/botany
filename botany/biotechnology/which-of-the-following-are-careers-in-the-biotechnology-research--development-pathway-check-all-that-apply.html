Which of the following are careers in the Biotechnology Research & Development Pathway? (CHECK ALL THAT APPLY)

(A) ** Biomedical Engineer

(B) Sports Medicine Physician

(C) Medical Illustrator

(D) ** Epidemilogist

(E) ** Forensic Science Technician (Criminalist)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The biotechnology research and development pathway includes occupations involved in bioscience research and development as it applies to human health. Workers may study diseases to discover new treatments or invent medical devices used to directly assist patients or to improve the accuracy of diagnostic tests.

Concept note-2: -biotechnology research and development tasks-some common task include the following:-gathering, recording, and analyzing data.-maintaining and using laboratory tools and equipment.-planning, organizing, and overseeing research studies and experiments.

Concept note-3: -Primary duties: Forensic science technicians work at crime scene investigations and are responsible for gathering and analysing evidence. They may take photos and keep written records of crime scene evidence. These professionals most commonly work for crime labs, police departments and medical examiner offices.
