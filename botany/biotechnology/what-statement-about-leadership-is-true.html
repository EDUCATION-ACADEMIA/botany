What statement about leadership is true?

(A) ** Leaders praise and give credit to others

(B) Effective leaders are born that way

(C) The president or chairperson is always the leader

(D) A leader is always chosen by others in an election

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The answer is d. It focuses on downward influence on followers. The act of leadership involves interaction and influence over subordinates.

Concept note-2: -Apr 23, 2022. A personal leadership statement is an important exercise in self-knowledge that clarifies a leader’s purpose, values, and beliefs about who they are as a leader and how they lead others. The process begins with identifying the core experiences and role models that have influenced their leadership.

Concept note-3: -I am not afraid of an army of lions led by a sheep; I am afraid of an army of sheep led by a lion. A leader is one who knows the way, goes the way, and shows the way. A leader is best when people barely know he exists, when his work is done, his aim fulfilled, they will say: we did it ourselves.

Concept note-4: -“Leaders are made, they are not born. They are made by hard effort, which is the price which all of us must pay to achieve any goal that is worthwhile.” “Outstanding leaders go out of the way to boost the self-esteem of their personnel. If people believe in themselves, it’s amazing what they can accomplish.”
