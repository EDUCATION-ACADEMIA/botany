BIOTECHNOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Jobs in the Biotechnology Research & Development Pathway typically require day-to-day interaction with patients.

(A) TRUE

(B) ** FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Careers in the Biotechnology Research and Development pathway involve bioscience research and development as it applies to human health. These scientists may study diseases to discover new treatments or invent medical devices used to directly assist patients or to improve the accuracy of diagnostic tests.

Concept note-2: -biotechnology research and development tasks-some common task include the following:-gathering, recording, and analyzing data.-maintaining and using laboratory tools and equipment.-planning, organizing, and overseeing research studies and experiments.
