What would a biotechnology engineer not work with?

(A) ** astronomy

(B) farming

(C) crime

(D) pharmaceuticals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You cannot, because role does not exist in the real world yet. Since there are no known alien life forms therefore, “Xenoarchaeology” only exists in science fiction. However, you might want to look into ‘astrobiology’ also known as ‘exobiology’.

Concept note-2: -There are quite a few biotechnologists that work at my NASA center, Ames Research Center, Moffett Field, California. We have a division that is named as Space Biosciences that has different folks who work on both science and technology aspects of space biology.

Concept note-3: -Yes, you can get a job in ISRO with MSc in Biotech. For that you first have to fulfill the eligibility criteria which is that one should have BSc and MSc in first class with an aggregate minimum of 65% marks.

Concept note-4: -Unfortunately the answer is NO.
