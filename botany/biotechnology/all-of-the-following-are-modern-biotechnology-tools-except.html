BIOTECHNOLOGY

[SOURCES]
All of the following are modern biotechnology tools except

(A) proteins

(B) cells

(C) DNA

(D) ** crystals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Modern biotechnologies involve making useful products from whole organisms or parts of organisms, such as molecules, cells, tissues and organs. Recent developments in biotechnology include genetically modified plants and animals, cell therapies and nanotechnology.

Concept note-2: -The three important techniques of biotechnology are: (1) Recombinant DNA Technology (Genetic Engineering) (2) Plant Tissue Culture and (3) Transgenic (Genetically Modified Organisms).

Concept note-3: -So, the correct option is ‘Making beer and wine’.
