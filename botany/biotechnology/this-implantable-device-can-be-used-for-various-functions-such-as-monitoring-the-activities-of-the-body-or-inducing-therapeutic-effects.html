(A) Biotech software

(B) ** Biological Computers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The biological computer is an implantable device that is mainly used for tasks like monitoring the bodyâ€™s activities or inducing therapeutic effects, all at the molecular or cellular level.

Concept note-2: -Biocomputing uses molecular biology parts as the hardware to implement computational devices. By following pre-defined rules, often hard-coded into biological systems, these devices are able to process inputs and return outputs-thus computing information.

Concept note-3: -Biocomputers use biologically derived materials to perform computational functions. A biocomputer consists of a pathway or series of metabolic pathways involving biological materials that are engineered to behave in a certain manner based upon the conditions (input) of the system.

Concept note-4: -Bioinformatics is the name given to these mathematical and computing approaches used to glean understanding of biological processes.
