Studying Biotechnology is least likely to be helpful for which career?

(A) Agricultural Researcher

(B) Pharmacist

(C) ** Auto Mechanic

(D) Livestock Breeder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DNA technology is important to both basic and applied (practical) biology. For instance, a technique used to make many copies of a DNA sequence, called polymerase chain reaction (PCR), is used in many medical diagnostic tests and forensics applications as well as in basic laboratory research.

Concept note-2: -The main subfields of biotechnology are medical (red) biotechnology, agricultural (green) biotechnology, industrial (white) biotechnology, marine (blue) biotechnology, food biotechnology, and environmental biotechnology (Fig. 1.1.).

Concept note-3: -Biotechnology encompasses three aspects of woody plant biology: (1) in vitro culture, (2) use of immunological methods to quantify plant metabolites, and (3) genetic engineering, that is, the transfer of genes between organisms without normal sexual reproduction.
