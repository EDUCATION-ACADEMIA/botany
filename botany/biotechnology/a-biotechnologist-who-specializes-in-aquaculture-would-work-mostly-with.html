A biotechnologist who specializes in aquaculture would work MOSTLY with:

(A) Plants.

(B) Bacteria.

(C) ** Fish.

(D) Birds.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The potential areas of biotechnology in aquaculture include the use of synthetic hormones in induced breeding, production of monosex, uniparental and polyploid population, molecular biology, transgenic fish, gene banking, improved feeds and health management and development of natural products from marine organisms.

Concept note-2: -The four main areas where biotechnologies have been used in aquaculture and fisheries include genetic improvements and control of reproduction; biosecurity and disease control; environmental management and bioremediation; and biodiversity conservation and fisheries management.

Concept note-3: -Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) is a molecular cytogenetic technique that allows the localization of a specific DNA sequence or an entire chromosome in a cell.
