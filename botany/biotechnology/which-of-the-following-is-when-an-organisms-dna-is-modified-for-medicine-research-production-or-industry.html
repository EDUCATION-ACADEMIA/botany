EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A genetically modified organism (GMO) is an animal, plant, or microbe whose DNA has been altered using genetic engineering techniques.

Concept note-2: -A genetically modified organism (GMO) is an organism whose DNA has been modified in the laboratory in order to favour the expression of desired physiological traits or the production of desired biological products.

Concept note-3: -GM is a technology that involves inserting DNA into the genome of an organism. To produce a GM plant, new DNA is transferred into plant cells. Usually, the cells are then grown in tissue culture where they develop into plants. The seeds produced by these plants will inherit the new DNA.

Concept note-4: -A transgenic, or genetically modified, organism is one that has been altered through recombinant DNA technology, which involves either the combining of DNA from different genomes or the insertion of foreign DNA into a genome.

Concept note-5: -Defining GMO Medicines GMO medicines are biological medicines which contain therapeutic genetic material which are used to treat disease. Locally, the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) defines GMO medicines as genetically modified organisms which are intended for use as medicinal agents.
