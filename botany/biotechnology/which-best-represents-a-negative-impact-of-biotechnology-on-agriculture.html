Which best represents a negative impact of biotechnology on agriculture

(A) cows increasing their milk production

(B) ** crop resistance to herbicides

(C) crop resistance to insect pests

(D) larger crop yield

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(iv) Agricultural management based on broad-spectrum herbicides as in herbicide-resistant crops further decreases diversity and abundance of wild plants and impacts arthropod fauna and other farmland animals.

Concept note-2: -use of biotechnological seeds may result in the loss of genetic diversity of crops. moving towards genetic homogeneity can lead to a higher susceptibility of plants to many pests, diseases or other negative environmental impacts.
