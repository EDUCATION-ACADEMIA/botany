What is an example of a life form used in industrial biotechnology?

(A) Android

(B) ** Bacteria

(C) Proton

(D) Oil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is an example of a life form used in industrial biotechnology? Environmental contaminants.

Concept note-2: -Advanced chemical manufacturing based on biological sources such as plants, algae, bacteria, yeast, filamentous fungi, and other organisms can replace many chemicals now derived from petroleum or other fossil fuels.

Concept note-3: -Biotechnology is defined as the application of knowledge in life sciences to create products or services that are beneficial to humans, being used to improve the quality and efficiency of food production, or even the production of cosmetic active ingredients, drugs, and vaccines [1].

Concept note-4: -Industrial microbiology is a branch of biotechnology that applies microbial sciences to create industrial products in mass quantities, often using microbial cell factories. There are multiple ways to manipulate a microorganism in order to increase maximum product yields.

Concept note-5: -Bioremediation is a branch of biotechnology that employs the use of living organisms, like microbes and bacteria to decontaminate affected areas. It is used in the removal of contaminants, pollutants, and toxins from soil, water, and other environments.
