Which statement is true of genetically altered foods?

(A) Genetically altered foods have been proven to be harmful for human consumption.

(B) No human should ever eat a genetically altered food product until further testing is done.

(C) All foods would be of more value if genetically altered, as they would not be subject to disease or weather hazards.

(D) ** Genetically altered foods can effectively increase the human food supply but should be clearly labeled, allowing free choice in consumption.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -So far, scientists have found no evidence that commercially available GMO foods are dangerous for health. Environmental concerns include the risk of altered genes entering wild species.

Concept note-3: -Genetically modified (GM) foods are foods derived from organisms whose genetic material (DNA) has been modified in a way that does not occur naturally, e.g. through the introduction of a gene from a different organism.
