Which sequence of DNA bases would pair with this partial strandATG TGA CAG

(A) ATG TGA CAG

(B) ** TAC ACT GTC

(C) GTA AGT GAC

(D) CAT TCA CTG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(c) Adenine always pairs with thymine and cytosine always pairs with guanine. Thus, sequence of bases on complementary strand would be TACGTACGT.

Concept note-2: -DNA base pair. Under normal circumstances, the nitrogen-containing bases adenine (A) and thymine (T) pair together, and cytosine (C) and guanine (G) pair together.

Concept note-3: -In DNA, there are four different bases: adenine (A), guanine (G), cytosine (C) and thymine (T). Each base has its complementary base, which means in the double helical structure of DNA, A will have T as its complimentary and similarly G will have C.
