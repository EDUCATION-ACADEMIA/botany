It is the transfer of genetic material for the purpose of treating human disease.

(A) chemotherapy

(B) ** gene therapy

(C) gene transfer

(D) gene editing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gene therapy is a technique that modifies a personâ€™s genes to treat or cure disease. Gene therapies can work by several mechanisms: Replacing a disease-causing gene with a healthy copy of the gene. Inactivating a disease-causing gene that is not functioning properly.

Concept note-2: -Human gene transfer (therapy) is the process of transferring genetic material (DNA or RNA) into a person. Gene therapy is a medical intervention based on modification of the genetic material of living cells which is then given to humans.

Concept note-3: -Gene therapy is used to correct defective genes in order to cure a disease or help your body better fight disease. Researchers are investigating several ways to do this, including: Replacing mutated genes. Some cells become diseased because certain genes work incorrectly or no longer work at all.

Concept note-4: -Conjugation is the process by which one bacterium transfers genetic material to another through direct contact. During conjugation, one bacterium serves as the donor of the genetic material, and the other serves as the recipient.

Concept note-5: -There are basically three types of gene therapy: ex vivo, in vivo, and in situ.
