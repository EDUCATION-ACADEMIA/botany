EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following statements about recombinant DNA technology is FALSE? It has limited application because genes of interest cannot be moved from one type of cell to another. This statement is FALSE. Recombinant DNA technology is commonly used to move DNA from one type of cell to another.

Concept note-3: -Taq DNA Polymerase is a highly thermostable recombinant DNA polymerase. It is named after Thermus aquaticus, the heat-tolerant bacterium from which it isolates itself. The molecular weight of the Taq Polymerase is 94kD, and it amplifies DNA up to 5kb with an elongation velocity of 0.9-1.2kb/min at 70-75°C.
