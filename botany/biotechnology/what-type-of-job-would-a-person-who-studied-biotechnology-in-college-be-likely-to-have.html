What type of job would a person who studied biotechnology in college be likely to have?

(A) ** A job making medicines

(B) A job studying stars.

(C) A job studying the physical features on Earth.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In addition to this, they standardize drug dozes, methods, and potency to help in the process of manufacturing & distribution of medicinal compounds and various drugs on a large scale.

Concept note-2: -Biotechnologists have a variety of fields in which they can choose to specialize in. Some of the popular career options for graduates include Biotech marketing, healthcare informatics, medical science liaison and quality assurance associate, among many others.

Concept note-3: -Biotechnology professionals often work in the medical industry, taking on roles in pharmaceutical manufacturing and medical equipment production. Graduates also work with the military in public and private sector positions. Read on to learn about five common industries that employ biotechnologists.

Concept note-4: -Biotechnologists create and improve products and processes for agriculture, medicine and conservation using biological organisms. They study the genetic, chemical and physical attributes of cells, tissues and organisms, and identify industrial uses for them.

Concept note-5: -It is easy to see how biotechnology can be used for medicinal purposes. Knowledge of the genetic makeup of our species, the genetic basis of heritable diseases, and the invention of technology to manipulate and fix mutant genes provides methods to treat the disease.
