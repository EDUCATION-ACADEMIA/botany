Which area of biotechnology is most likely to create ethical problems?

(A) creating insulin through bacteria

(B) ** cloning organs

(C) genetic engineering crops to increase how much is grown (yield)

Concept note-1: -Ethical issues specific to human cloning include: the safety and efficacy of the procedure, cloning for destructive embryonic stem cell research, the effects of reproductive cloning on the child/parent relationship, and the commodification of human life as a research product.
