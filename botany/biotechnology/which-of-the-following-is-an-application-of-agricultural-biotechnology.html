Which of the following is an application of agricultural biotechnology?

(A) genetic testing and screening

(B) vaccine therapy

(C) ** transgenic crops

(D) genetically engineered enzymes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The six applications are: (1) Resistance to Biotic Stresses (2) Resistance to Abiotic Stresses (3) Improvement of Crop Yield and Quality (4) Transgenic Plants with Improved Nutrition(5) Commercial Transgenic Crop Plants and (6) Transgenic Plants as Bioreactors.

Concept note-2: -Biotechnology is a branch of science that develops or creates products using biological systems or components of them. Applications of biotechnology in the field of agriculture: Recombinant DNA technology is used to produce genetically modified products that produce a high yield in agriculture.

Concept note-3: -Some benefits of genetic engineering in agriculture are increased crop yields, reduced costs for food or drug production, reduced need for pesticides, enhanced nutrient composition and food quality, resistance to pests and disease, greater food security, and medical benefits to the worldâ€™s growing population.
