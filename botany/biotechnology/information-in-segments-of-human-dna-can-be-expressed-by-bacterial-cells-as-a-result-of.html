[SOURCES]
Information in segments of human DNA can be expressed by bacterial cells as a result of

(A) sexual reproduction

(B) random mutation

(C) genetic variability

(D) ** genetic engineering

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The genetic engineering process A small piece of circular DNA called a plasmid? is extracted from the bacteria or yeast cell.

Concept note-2: -The genetic material of bacteria and plasmids is DNA. Bacterial viruses (bacteriophages or phages) have DNA or RNA as genetic material. The two essential functions of genetic material are replication and expression.

Concept note-3: -Researchers can insert DNA fragments or genes into a plasmid vector, creating a so-called recombinant plasmid. This plasmid can be introduced into a bacterium by way of the process called transformation. Then, because bacteria divide rapidly, they can be used as factories to copy DNA fragments in large quantities.

Concept note-4: -Segments of DNA that carry genetic information are called genes, and they are inherited by offspring from their parents during reproduction.
