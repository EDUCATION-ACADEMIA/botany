Which is a widely used bio-indicator species used to detect air that is badly polluted with sulfur dioxide?

(A) E. coli

(B) bacteria

(C) ** lichens

(D) viruses

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lichens have also been used in Germany as a bioindicator for air pollution, as they are sensitive to a number of air pollutants. Specifically, lichens are the most sensitive to sulfur dioxide, but other acidic pollutant gases as well as heavy metals and photo oxidants.

Concept note-2: -Lichens are very sensitive to sulphur dioxide pollution in the air. Since industrialization, many lichen species have become extinct in large areas. Lichens are widely used as environmental indicators or bio-indicators.

Concept note-3: -Final answer: Lichens are the best indicator of SO.

Concept note-4: -Lichens are plants that can be used as natural indicators of air pollution, as they inform us about the concentration of sulphur dioxide in the air. They usually grow on rocks, and tree barks.

Concept note-5: -White pine, shade tobacco, mosses, and lichens have proven particularly useful as bioassay tools. Pollen from pollutant-sensitive and pollutant-tolerant plant cultivars has also been used as a sensitive laboratory bioassay tool for studying air quality.
