The instrument used to analyze Hair & Fiber Evidence ____

(A) ** Microscope

(B) Telescope

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Microspectrophotometers are used for spectral analysis of hairs and fibers by the cosmetics industry and forensic analysis.

Concept note-2: -What microscope can see human hair? A light microscope is typically used to view human hair. If a scientist would like to compare two samples, a comparison light microscope is typically used.

Concept note-3: -Hair samples are primarily collected using tweezers. Fiber-Fibers are threadlike elements from fabric or other materials such as carpet. Most are easily identifiable under a microscope.

Concept note-4: -A comparison microscope can be used to compare a questioned hair to a known hair sample in order to determine if the hairs are similar and if they could have come from a common source.

Concept note-5: -Hair and Fibers Fiber types can be determined using optical microscopy techniques. Human hair can be distinguished from animal hair using a Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM).
