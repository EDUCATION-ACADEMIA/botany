Which of the following is not the main step in carrying out plant breeding technique?

(A) Collection of variability

(B) ** Cross hybridization among the diseased parents

(C) Selection and testing of superior recombinants

(D) Evaluation and selection of parents

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the correct answer is ‘Inbreeding’

Concept note-2: -Plant Breeding Steps Variability forms the root of all breeding practices. The first step involves the collection of plants or seeds for all possible alleles for all genes in a given crop, which is known as germplasm. This collection even involves wild varieties and relatives of the cultivated species.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is mutation breeding: Plant breeding is the technique used to improve the variety of plants for human welfare. Introduction, selection, hybridization and mutation breeding are the main methods of crop improvement along with polyploid breeding, tissue culture and genetic engineering.

Concept note-4: -Final answer: It is independent of a germplasm collection is not correct regarding plant breeding.
