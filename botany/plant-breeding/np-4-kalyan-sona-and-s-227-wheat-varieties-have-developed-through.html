NP-4, kalyan sona and s-227 wheat varieties have developed through

(A) r-ray irradiation

(B) Multiple crossing

(C) ** Selection

(D) Genetic engineering

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sonalika and Kalyan Sona are the high-yielding and disease-resistant varieties of wheat which were introduced all over the wheat-growing belt of India in 1963. Sonalika and Kalyan Sona are the semi-dwarf varieties of wheat, popularly known as Mexican wheat, produced by Dr. Borlaug in Mexico in 1963.

Concept note-2: -Kalyan Sona is high yielding, double dwarf wheat with wide adaptation recommended for cultivation all over India. So, the correct option is C. ( High yielding variety of wheat )

Concept note-3: -Sonalika and Kalyan Sona are high yielding and disease resistant. No worries!
