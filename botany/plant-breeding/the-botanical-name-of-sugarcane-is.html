The botanical name of Sugarcane is:

(A) Oryza sativum

(B) ** Saccharum barberi

(C) Pisum Sativum

(D) Triticum

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The botanical name of sugarcane is Saccharum officinarum.

Concept note-2: -sugarcane, (Saccharum officinarum), perennial grass of the family Poaceae, primarily cultivated for its juice from which sugar is processed.

Concept note-3: -Reason : Saccharum barberi is also called tropical cane.

Concept note-4: -Saccharum barberi is grown in North India but is poor in sugar content. Saccharum officinarum is a South Indian species of sugarcane with thick stems and higher sugar content but does not grow well in North India.

Concept note-5: -The botanical name of sugar is Saccharum officinarum.
