When the plant grows away from the ____ stimuli

(A) Positive

(B) ** Negative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Negative tropism is when a plant moves away from a stimulus.

Concept note-2: -3. Vocabulary tropism plant growth toward or away from a stimulus. phototropism. the response of a plant to light; stems bend toward the light, roots. move away from the light.

Concept note-3: -Plant parts can grow with or against gravity. This type of tropism is called gravitropism. Roots of a plant grow downward and exhibit positive gravitropism. Stems, on the other hand, exhibit negative gravitropism since they grow upwards and against the force of gravity (see Figure 1).

Concept note-4: -Growth in the direction of a stimulus is known as positive tropism, while growth away from a stimulus is known as a negative tropism.

Concept note-5: -Environmental factors, including light, chemical nutrients, water, and gravity are stimuli that can provoke tropisms in a plant. The plant grows in the direction of the stimulus as hormones inside the stem, root, and leaf systems in a plant aid in the elongation and growth process of the plant toward the stimuli.
