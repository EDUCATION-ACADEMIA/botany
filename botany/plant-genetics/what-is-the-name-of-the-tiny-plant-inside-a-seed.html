What is the name of the tiny plant inside a seed?

(A) Nectar

(B) Fruit

(C) Stem

(D) ** Embryo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, a small baby plant coming out of a seed is called a seedling.

Concept note-2: -Embryo: 4. A baby plant is present within the seed coat known as an embryo. 5. An embryo consists of three parts: plumule, radicle, and cotyledons.

Concept note-3: -A fertilized ovule develops into a seed. Seed has the embryo within it which contains a plumule and radicle. Embryo is formed from the zygote which is formed when the egg is fertilized by the male gamete. Plumule and the radicle develops into a shoot and the root, respectively.

Concept note-4: -Cotyledons are the first leaves produced by plants. Cotyledons are not considered true leaves and are sometimes referred to as “seed leaves, ” because they are actually part of the seed or embryo of the plant.
