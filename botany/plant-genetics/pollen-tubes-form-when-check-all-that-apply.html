Pollen tubes form when (check all that apply)

(A) ** Pollen germinates

(B) Insects carry pollen to the stigma

(C) ** Enzymes digest through the style

(D) Pollen nucleus combines with egg nucleus

(E) The anther releases its pollen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Above the ovary, the style represents a support structure to which the stigma is attached at the top of the pistil. The stigma receives the male gametes contained within the pollen and releases a chemical signal causing a specialized cell within the pollen grain to form the pollen tube.

Concept note-2: -Pollen-specific pectin methylesterase (PME), a cell wall-modifying enzyme, was reported to enhance pollen tube tip dynamics and growth (Bosch and Hepler, 2005; Bosch et al., 2005; Jiang et al., 2005).

Concept note-3: -Hint: Pollen tube is a part of the male reproductive organ while the style is carried by the female reproductive organ. Pollen tubes and styles are parts of plants. Both pollen tubes and styles help in the reproduction process.
