Which of the following plants has a tap root system?

(A) Maize

(B) Wheat

(C) ** Rose

(D) Sugarcane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hibiscus and banyan are plants that have tap roots. In the tap root system, there is the main root from which other small roots arise.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is A True. Fibrous root system: Plants with fibrous root systems have clusters of roots arising from the base of the stem and are of equal length. For example, grass, maize and rose.

Concept note-3: -Final answer: Name of plant is rose Root system is fibrous root.

Concept note-4: -Taproots are commonly found in plants like beetroot, burdock, carrot, sugar beet, dandelion, parsley, parsnip, poppy mallow, radish, sagebrush, turnip, common milkweed, cannabis, and trees such as oaks, elms, pines, and firs are some of the taproot plant names.

Concept note-5: -Mustard, carrot, beetroot, parsley, china rose and all dicotyledons are examples of taproot systems. Fibrous roots, on the other hand, are bushy roots in which thin, moderately branching roots grow from the stem.
