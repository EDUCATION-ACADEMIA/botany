PLANT MORPHOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Aestivation is arrangement of

(A) seeds inside the fruit with respect to other members

(B) leaves on the stem

(C) flowers on the floral axis with respect to other members

(D) ** sepals / petals in floral bud with respect to other members of the same whorl

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aestivation is the arrangement of either the sepals or the petals in a flower bud with relation to one another as vernation proper is the arrangement of young leaves in the vegetative bud. This is a very important characteristic in the classification of plants.

Concept note-2: -Aestivation is the mode of arrangement of sepals or petals with respect to the other members of the same whorl.

Concept note-3: -Vexillary aestivation is a special type of aestivation. In valvate aestivation margins of adjacent petals or sepals touch each other at the margin without overlapping.

Concept note-4: -The arrangement of floral leaves in a floral bud is known as aestivation. It occurs in different floral parts like calyx and corolla. The arrangement of leaves in a vegetative bud is known as prefoliation.

Concept note-5: -The mode of arrangement of sepals or petals in floral bud with respect to the other members of the same whorl is known as aestivation.
