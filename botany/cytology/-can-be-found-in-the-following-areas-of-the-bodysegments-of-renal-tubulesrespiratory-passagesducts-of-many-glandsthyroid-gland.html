____ can be found in the following areas of the body:*segments of renal tubules*respiratory passages*ducts of many glands*thyroid gland

(A) ** Simple cuboidal epithelium

(B) simple squamous epithelium

(C) Simple columnar epithelium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cuboidal epithelium cells are present in the lining of kidney tubules and ducts of salivary glands. It helps in protection, absorption, excretion and secretion.

Concept note-2: -Types of epithelial cells in your body Simple cuboidal epithelium: This type of epithelium is typically found in glandular (secreting) tissue and kidney tubules.

Concept note-3: -The cuboidal epithelium is composed of a single layer of cube-like cells. This is commonly found in ducts of glands and tubular parts of nephrons in kidneys and its main functions are secretion and absorption.

Concept note-4: -Cuboidal epithelium typically lines ducts draining glands.
