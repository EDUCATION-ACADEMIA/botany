Which answer best describes eukaryotic cells?

(A) A cell that does photosynthesis.

(B) A cell that is no longer alive.

(C) ** A cell with a true nucleus and organelles

(D) A cell that has a cell wall.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answers: Eukaryotic cells have membrane-bound organelles and have a true nucleus whereas prokaryotic cells do not have a true nucleus. The prokaryotic cells have a nucleoid region where the chromosome lies. Hence, the correct answer is ‘Do not have a nucleus’.

Concept note-2: -eukaryote, any cell or organism that possesses a clearly defined nucleus. The eukaryotic cell has a nuclear membrane that surrounds the nucleus, in which the well-defined chromosomes (bodies containing the hereditary material) are located.

Concept note-3: -A eukaryotic cell is a cell that has a membrane-bound nucleus and other membrane-bound compartments or sacs, called organelles, which have specialized functions. The word eukaryotic means “true kernel” or “true nucleus, ” alluding to the presence of the membrane-bound nucleus in these cells.

Concept note-4: -Eukaryotic cells are those that contain a membrane-bound nucleus and membrane-bound organelles. All plants, animals, fungi, and protists are eukaryotes. The membrane-bound nucleus is an organelle.

Concept note-5: -Eukaryotes are organisms whose cells contain a nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles. There is a wide range of eukaryotic organisms, including all animals, plants, fungi, and protists, as well as most algae.
