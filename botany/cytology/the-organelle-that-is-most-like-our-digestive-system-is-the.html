The organelle that is most like our digestive system is the?

(A) Endoplasmic Reticulum

(B) ** Lysosomes

(C) Nucleus

(D) Cytoplasm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lysosomes function as the digestive system of the cell, serving both to degrade material taken up from outside the cell and to digest obsolete components of the cell itself.

Concept note-2: -A lysosome is a membrane-bound cell organelle that contains digestive enzymes. Lysosomes are involved with various cell processes. They break down excess or worn-out cell parts.

Concept note-3: -Stomach: Stomach is a muscular organ that secretes enzymes to aid in the digestion of food. Stomach function similar to the lysosome.

Concept note-4: -Lysosomes are a kind of waste disposal system of a cell. They help to keep the cell clean by digesting any foreign material as well as worn-out cell organelles. Lysosomes contain powerful digestive enzymes capable of breaking down all organic material, so they are called digestive bags of the cell.

Concept note-5: -Lysosomes are an important cell organelle found within eukaryotic animal cells. Due to their peculiar function, they are also known as the “suicide bags” of the cell.
