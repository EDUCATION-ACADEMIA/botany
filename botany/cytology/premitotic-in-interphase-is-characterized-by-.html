Premitotic in interphase is characterized by ____

(A) ** synthesis of RNA, ATP, tubulins

(B) reduplication of DNA

(C) synthesis of the enzymes that are necessary for the formation of RNA precursors

(D) temporary or definitive loss of the ability for division

(E) division of the initial cell into two

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Interphase is composed of G1 phase (cell growth), followed by S phase (DNA synthesis), followed by G2 phase (cell growth).

Concept note-2: -G2 phase: The interval between the end of DNA synthesis and the beginning of mitosis is known as the gap 2 phase; during this phase, the RNA, tubulin, and additional proteins required for cell division are synthesized.

Concept note-3: -During the cell cycle, RNA and protein synthesis takes place during the G1 and G2 phases. The cell cycle is an ordered set of events, culminating in cell growth and division into two daughter cells.

Concept note-4: -The interphase is comprised of first growth phase or G1 phase, synthesis phase or S phase and second growth phase or G2 phase. In G1 phase, there is synthesis of RNA, enzymes, proteins, amino acids for histone formation, formation of nucleotides and ATP.
