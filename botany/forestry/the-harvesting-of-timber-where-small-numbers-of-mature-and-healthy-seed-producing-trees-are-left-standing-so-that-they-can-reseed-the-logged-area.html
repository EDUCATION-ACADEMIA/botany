BOTANY

FORESTRY

[SOURCES]
The harvesting of timber where small numbers of mature and healthy seed-producing trees are left standing so that they can reseed the logged area

(A) ** seed tree approach

(B) uneven planting

(C) even planting

(D) classic tree approach

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clearcutting reduces biodiversity. Because clearcutting indiscriminately destroys entire areas, plant loss and destruction of animal habitat is unavoidable. Further environmental stress is put on many already endangered species that call the forest home.

Concept note-2: -Adaptive Management. A customized approach to managing resources that has been developed through scientific testing.

Concept note-3: -The goal of “maximum sustainable yield management” is a harvest resources in ways that minimize impact on the ecosystems and ecological processes that provide the resource.

Concept note-4: -As you might suspect by its name, the selective cutting system is the least destructive logging method. The primary difference about selective cutting is that it only removes trees of a specific type, size (or age), and quality.
