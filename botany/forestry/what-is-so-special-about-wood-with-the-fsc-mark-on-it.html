What is so special about wood with the FSC mark on it

(A) It is more expensive

(B) It comes from rare trees

(C) It was produced by a well know company

(D) ** the wood came from a sustainable source

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sustainable production FSC certification ensures that the wood comes from forests managed according to high environmental, social and economic standards. This ensures that we can benefit from services and products today without compromising these functions for future generations.

Concept note-2: -As the leader in sustainable forestry, FSC is trusted to protect forests for all, forever. Every FSC label stands for sustainable sourcing that puts forests and people first.

Concept note-3: -The Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) is an international, non-governmental organisation dedicated to promoting responsible management of the world’s forests. Since its foundation in 1994, FSC has grown to become the world’s most respected and widespread forest certification system.

Concept note-4: -Sustainably sourced wood minimizes the impact of logging on forests and the surrounding wildlife and communities. Sustainable wood also means that the timber is not a product of illegal logging that violates national and/or regional regulations protecting forests and the environment.
