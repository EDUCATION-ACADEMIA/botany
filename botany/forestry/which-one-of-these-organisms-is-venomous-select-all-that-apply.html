Which one of these organisms is venomous? Select all that apply

(A) Poison Ivy

(B) False morel mushrooms

(C) ** Brown Recluse spider

(D) ** Copperhead snake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poisonous snakes include vipers, rattlesnakes, cobras, kraits, mambas, and the American coral snake.

Concept note-2: -The northern copperhead is a large, social, venomous snake found across the eastern United States in terrestrial and semiaquatic habitats.

Concept note-3: -Snake venom can be classified into three major categories – neurotoxic, hemotoxic and cytotoxic. These venoms impact specific regions and functions of the target’s body.

Concept note-4: -It is usually coppery or reddish brown. It is dangerous but is unaggressive when left alone. The copperhead of India is a rat snake, Elaphe radiata.
