Last June 4-5, a virtual gala event organized by the UN Environment Programme was held in Pakistan to launch which UN Decade celebration?

(A) United Nations Decade on Ecosystem Resilience

(B) United Nations Decade on Biodiversity and Ecosystem

(C) ** United Nations Decade on Ecosystem Restoration

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan will act as global host of the day. World Environment Day 2021 will see the launch of the UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration. OFFICIAL BROADCAST 4-5 June: Watch the UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration Launch and the host country celebrations by Pakistan.

Concept note-2: -The UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration aims to prevent, halt and reverse the degradation of ecosystems on every continent and in every ocean. It can help to end poverty, combat climate change and prevent a mass extinction. It will only succeed if everyone plays a part.

Concept note-3: -Purpose. With more lands around the world facing increasing deterioration and degradation, the United Nations General Assembly declared the United Nations Decade for Deserts and the Fight Against Desertification, which runs from January 2010 to December 2020 to promote action that will protect the drylands.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations General Assembly declared 2021–2030 as the UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration with the main target of preventing, halting and reversing degraded, damaged and destroyed ecosystems worldwide.

Concept note-5: -The UN Decade runs from 2021 through 2030, which is also the deadline for the Sustainable Development Goals and the timeline scientists have identified as the last chance to prevent catastrophic climate change.
