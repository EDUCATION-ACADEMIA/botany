Which of the following is a threat to forest ecosystems?

(A) climate change

(B) unsustainable logging

(C) invasive species

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ecosystem degradation drivers such as (1) deforestation, (2) fragmentation and (3) forest fire play a vital role in the forest degradation.

Concept note-2: -Natural disasters and disturbances Natural disturbances are interacting with climate change to further increase forest degradation. Climate change is enabling invasive plant and insect species to gain advantage over native species.

Concept note-3: -Threats to our forests Some are natural factors such as fire, insects, disease, or wind and ice storms. Others, such as climate change, invasive species and forestland conversion, are a result of human activity.

Concept note-4: -Major factors leading to forest loss are habitat loss and degradation, invasive alien species, overuse of resources, and pollution.

Concept note-5: -The main cause of deforestation is agriculture (poorly planned infrastructure is emerging as a big threat too) and the main cause of forest degradation is illegal logging.
