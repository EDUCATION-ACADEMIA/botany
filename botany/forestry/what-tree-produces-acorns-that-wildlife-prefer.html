(A) White Pine

(B) ** White Oak

Concept note-1: -4. Swamp Chestnut Oak. Swamp chestnut oak also produces preferred acorns being a member of the white oak section.

Concept note-2: -Water Oak The water oak produces high yields of acorns. So while the taste is bitter, the abundance is there and the acorns will help carry deer through the winter months. This is critical when acorns from the white oak group are long gone.
