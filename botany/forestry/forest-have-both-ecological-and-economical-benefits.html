Forest have both ecological and economical benefits

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Forests provide us with shelter, livelihoods, water, food and fuel security. All these activities directly or indirectly involve forests. Some are easy to figure out-fruits, paper and wood from trees, and so on.

Concept note-2: -Healthy forest ecosystems produce and conserve soil and stabilize stream flows and water runoff-preventing land degradation and desertification, and reducing the risks of natural disasters such as droughts, floods, and landslides.

Concept note-3: -Forest is significance for surviving living organism because it provides all benefits free of cost forest provides all kind of ecosystem services like Carbon sequestration, Soil conservation, Purification of water, Maintain local climate and Biodiversity support etc.

Concept note-4: -Forests provide a wide range of economic and social benefits to humankind. These include contributions to the overall economy – for example through employment, processing and trade of forest products and energy – and investments in the forest sector.
