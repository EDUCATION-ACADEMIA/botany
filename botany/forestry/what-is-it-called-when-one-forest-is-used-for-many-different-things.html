What is it called when one forest is used for many different things?

(A) ** Multiple Use Management

(B) Selective Succession

(C) Clearing

(D) Multiple Silviculture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“the management of land or forest for more than one purpose, such as wood production, water quality, wildlife, recreation, aesthetics, or clean air”.

Concept note-2: -To maintain or improve the health and productivity of a forest and to achieve the landowner’s objectives for the property, foresters use a number of management techniques, including harvesting, prescribed burning and reforestation.
