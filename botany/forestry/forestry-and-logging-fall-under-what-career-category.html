Forestry and logging fall under what career category?

(A) ** Agricultural

(B) Business

(C) Industrial

(D) Educational

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Primary sector contains agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, fishing, farming and mining activities. Those activities that make direct use of natural resources are enlisted in the Primary sector. Q.

Concept note-2: -It is neither branch of agriculture nor forestry. Agriculture is science of food production amd forestry is science and art of wood production. Where as agroforestry is science of food and wood production on a unit of land .

Concept note-3: -The four types of agriculture are as follows: Pastoralism – Pastoralism involves herding of animals. Shifting Cultivation – It involves rotation of crops. Subsistence farming – It is a practice of growing crops and raising livestock sufficient only for one’s own use, without any surplus for trad.

Concept note-4: -The important sectors consider include Agriculture, Horticulture, Animal husbandry, Fishery, Soil and water conservation, Poultry, dairy farming, Agriculture Marketing, Milk-fed financial institute and Social forestry.
