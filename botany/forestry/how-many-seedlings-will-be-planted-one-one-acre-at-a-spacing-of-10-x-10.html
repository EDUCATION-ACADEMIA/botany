How many seedlings will be planted one one acre at a spacing of 10’ x 10’

(A) 302

(B) ** 436

(C) 889

(D) 1, 742

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can grow 200 trees on 1 acre-The Hindu.

Concept note-2: -Suggested small grain seeding rates for grain drills with different row spacing. These seeding rates are based on 85 percent emergence of planted seed. Plant about 1.1 million to 1.6 million seeds per acre (about 75 to 125 pounds of seed per acre) with a grain drill.

Concept note-3: -Multiply the square footage of the planting bed by the plants needed per square foot to determine the number of plants needed. When using plants with different spacing requirements in the same area, estimate the square footage you will need for each plant. Number of Plants per Sq. Ft.
