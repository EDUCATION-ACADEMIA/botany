TRUE or FALSE.Kaladis narig is a tropical hardwood tree species endemic to the Philippines that can only be found in the forests of Zamboanga Sibugay.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Narra. Known as the national tree of the country, the Narra tree is a native tree found in primary and secondary forests at low and medium altitudes throughout the country.

Concept note-2: -The primary species grown in the Philippines are tropical hardwoods Acacia Falcata, Acacia Mangium, Mahogany, and Gmelina Yemane. The southern part of the country produces the majority of the lumber, veneer, and plywood.

Concept note-3: -Among the most important are molave; narra, Pterocarpus spp.; tindalo, Pahudia rhomboidea; ipil, Intsia bijuga; akle, Albizzia acle; and banuyo, Wallaceodendron celebicum. Pine-type forests occur in the high mountainous regions of northern Luzon and Mindoro.
