A broad scoop attached to the handle. It is used to dig, lift, and throw loose matter.

(A) Axe

(B) Fork

(C) Trowel

(D) ** Shovel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shovel is a tool used to dig as well as to move loose, granular materials (like dirt, gravel, grain, or snow) from one spot to another.

Concept note-2: -A shovel is used as a tool for lifting and digging soil. It also looks like a spade.

Concept note-3: -From shovels and spades, to augers and post hole diggers.

Concept note-4: -Scoop Shovel They are useful for moving large amounts of material, including soil, gravel, wood chippings, and debris.
