[SOURCES]
Which of the following statements are TRUE about plant propagation using seeds?

(A) ** If the seeds are high in number, direct seeding on plant bed can be done without worrying seeds run out.

(B) ** Plant produced from seeds, might not be the same like the mother plant.

(C) ** The seeds are best sown in seed tray if the number of seeds are limited.

(D) All plant seeds can be stored for long period of time as long as they are kept in refrigerator.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final Answer: The true statements for sexual reproduction in plants are that the plants are obtained from seeds and fertilization can occur only after pollination.

Concept note-2: -Vegetative propagation is a form of asexual reproduction in plants. Only one plant is involved and the offspring is the result of one parent. The new plant is genetically identical to the parent and they grow from parts of the parent plant.

Concept note-3: -Stem Cuttings: This technique is probably the most versatile of all methods used for vegetative propagation. It can be used for both herbaceous and woody material. Herbaceous stem cuttings can be made from houseplants, annual flowers and bedding plants, ground covers, and some perennials.

Concept note-4: -The major methods of asexual propagation are cuttings, layering, division, budding and grafting. Cuttings involve rooting a severed piece of the parent plant; layering involves rooting a part of the parent and then severing it; and budding and grafting is joining two plant parts from different varieties.
