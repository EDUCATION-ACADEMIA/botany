An advantage of asexual reproduction is that it does not require fertilization. What advantage do the offspring of sexual reproduction most likely have as a result of fertilization?

(A) ** The DNA variation in offspring may allow survival in a changing environment.

(B) The DNA of the offspring is the same as the parent.

(C) The offspring must use energy to find a mate.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is (b) Sexual reproduction provides more genetic variability. Sexual reproduction produces genetically diverse offspring from two separate parents, resulting in more genetic variability.
