Which of the following plant parts is not used in asexual propagation?

(A) stems

(B) roots

(C) leaves

(D) ** seeds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asexual reproduction in plants occurs through budding, fragmentation, vegetative propagation, and spore formation. No flowers are required for this method.

Concept note-2: -So, the correct answer is ‘antherozoid’

Concept note-3: -Asexual propagation involves the vegetative parts of a plant: stems, roots, or leaves.

Concept note-4: -There are two general types of propagation: sexual and asexual. Sexual propagation is the reproduction of plants by seeds. The genetic material of two parents is combined by pollination and fertilization to create offspring that are different from each parent.
