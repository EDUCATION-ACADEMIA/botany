Which of the following statements are the applications of suspension culture? I. The production of mutants. II. The production of artificial seeds. III. The production of genetic modified products. IV. The production of secondary metabolites.

(A) I and II

(B) I and III

(C) I, II and III

(D) ** All of the above.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Accordingly, cell suspension cultures for commercial use have been studied recently. Examples of uses for these cultures include secondary metabolite production for drugs, flavorings and fragrances, and micropropagation, including the production of somatic embryos.

Concept note-2: -Which of the following is the main application of embryo culture? Sol: (d) Overcoming hybridisation barriers.

Concept note-3: -Applications include: micropropagation using meristem and shoot culture to produce large numbers of identical individuals. screening programmes of cells, rather than plants for advantageous characters. large-scale growth of plant cells in liquid culture as a source of secondary products.

Concept note-4: -Techniques to measure the growth in suspension culture: cell counting, packed cell volume, cell fresh weight, cell dry weight.
