Which of the following statements refer to the advantages of micropropagation? I. Production of plants in large scale rapidly. II. Plants produced are free from diseases/pathogens. III. Plants can be produced throughout the year. IV. Plants produced normally high degree of uniformity (true to type) compared to conventional breeding.

(A) I, II and III

(B) I, II and IV

(C) II, III and IV

(D) ** All of the above.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Following are the various advantages of tissue culture technique: The plantlets are obtained in a very short time with a small amount of plant tissue. The new plants produced are disease-free. The plants can be grown throughout the year, irrespective of the season.

Concept note-2: -The final answer: A technique of micropropagation is Somatic embryogenesis.
