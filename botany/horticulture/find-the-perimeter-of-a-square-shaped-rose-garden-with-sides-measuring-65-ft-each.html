Find the perimeter of a square shaped rose garden with sides measuring 6.5 ft each.

(A) 13.0 ft

(B) 19.5 ft

(C) 42.25 ft

(D) ** 26.0 ft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Perimeter of a square = sum of length of all sides. lengths..so, 6.4 X 4 = 25.6 cm. The perimeter of the square is 25.6 cm.

Concept note-2: -Therefore, perimeter of a regular octagon of side 6 . 5 c m is 52 c m .

Concept note-3: -The perimeter of a square is the total length of all the sides of the square. Hence, we can find the perimeter of a square by adding all its four sides. All the sides of a square are equal. So, the perimeter of a square is calculated by adding the side of a square four times.

Concept note-4: -Since all the sides of a square are equal, therefore, the perimeter of square will be 4 times its side, i.e. 4 × Side.
