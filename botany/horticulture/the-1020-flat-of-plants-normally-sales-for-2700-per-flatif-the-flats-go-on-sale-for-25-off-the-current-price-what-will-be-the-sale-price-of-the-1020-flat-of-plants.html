The 1020 flat of plants normally sales for $27.00 per flat.If the flats go on sale for 25% off the current price, what will be the sale price of the 1020 flat of plants?

(A) $6.75

(B) ** $20.25

(C) $22.00

(D) $26.75

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tip the flat on end and let the plants begin to fall out and then separate them by simply pulling them apart; this preserves most of the roots. If the roots are really knitted together, cut the plants out like pieces of cake, using an ordinary spatula or putty knife.

Concept note-2: -In order to have the number of rows and number of columns equal, the number should be a perfect square. Hence, the number of additional plants required are 24.
