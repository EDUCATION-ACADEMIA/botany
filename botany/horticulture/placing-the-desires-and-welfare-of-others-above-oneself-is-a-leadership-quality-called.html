Placing the desires and welfare of others above oneself is a leadership quality called:

(A) enthusiasm

(B) parliamentary procedure

(C) tact

(D) ** unselfishness

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Placing the desires and welfare of others above yourself is a leadership quality called: Unselfishness. The leadership quality that means reliable support for an individual, group or cause is: Loyalty.

Concept note-2: -Behavior is normally described as altruistic when it is motivated by a desire to benefit someone other than oneself for that person’s sake. The term is used as the contrary of “self-interested” or “selfish” or “egoistic”-words applied to behavior that is motivated solely by the desire to benefit oneself.

Concept note-3: -The first general purpose that some people have for giving speeches is to inform. Simply put, this is about helping audience members acquire information that they do not already possess.

Concept note-4: -Components of Instructional Program. 1. Supervised Agricultural Experience (SAE)-provides opportunities to explore interests and to gain work experiences.
