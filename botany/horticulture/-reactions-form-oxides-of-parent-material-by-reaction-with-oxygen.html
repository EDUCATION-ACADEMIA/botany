____ reactions form oxides of parent material by reaction with oxygen.

(A) Hydrolysis

(B) ** Oxidation

(C) Aseptic

(D) Septic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Metals react with oxygen to form basic oxides. Metallic oxides are basic in nature because they react with dilute acids to form salt and water.

Concept note-2: -Oxidation is the gain of oxygen. Reduction is the loss of oxygen.

Concept note-3: -Many metals react with oxygen to form metal oxides. Potassium and sodium are soft metal which are easily cut exposing a shiny surface which changes to dull rapidly. The change from shiny to dull is called tarnishing.

Concept note-4: -combustion, a chemical reaction between substances, usually including oxygen and usually accompanied by the generation of heat and light in the form of flame.
