form flowers used in floral designs have:

(A) florets on tall spikes

(B) flowers on end of stems

(C) small sprays of flowers

(D) ** unique shapes or colors

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Form flower – Any plant material whose shape is the most distinctive feature, such as an iris. Line flower – A spike or spike-like inflorescence with an elongated stem, such as gladiolus, liatris, and larkspur. Mass flower – A single rounded flower at the top of a stem used for adding mass to a design.

Concept note-2: -The third element is form. Form includes three things height, width and depth. It is important to think about form while placing flowers because this is what determines the form. The fourth element is space, spacing of flowers give the viewer a chance to view each individual flower.

Concept note-3: -The basic shapes for floral design are round and triangular. From there the symmetrical triangle, asymmetrical triangle, round and oblong designs are derived.

Concept note-4: -The round flower arrangement is by far the most popular wedding or home décor flower arrangement. It is completely even distribution of flowers around all sides, inexpensive, and easy to make! Round flower arrangements are traditional and super easy.
