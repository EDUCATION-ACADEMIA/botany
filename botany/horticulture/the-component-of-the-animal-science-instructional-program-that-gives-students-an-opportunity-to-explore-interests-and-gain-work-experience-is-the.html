The component of the animal science instructional program that gives students an opportunity to explore interests and gain work experience is the:

(A) classroom

(B) FFA

(C) state ffa convention

(D) ** Supervised Agricultural Experience

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Components of Instructional Program. 1. Supervised Agricultural Experience (SAE)-provides opportunities to explore interests and to gain work experiences.

Concept note-2: -What component of the small animal instructional program has a major purpose the development of communication skills and confidence through competitive events? FFA. The leadership quality of honesty is called: Integrity.

Concept note-3: -Research. In a research SAE students plan and conduct major agricultural experiments using the scientific process and discover new knowledge. As part of the research, students verify and demonstrate or learn about scientific principles in agriculture. Research SAEs can be entrepreneurial or placement.

Concept note-4: -There are four types of supervised agricultural experience programs: exploratory, entrepre-neurship, placement, and research/experimentation. Each student’s SAE is based on the stu-dent’s interests and available opportunities.
