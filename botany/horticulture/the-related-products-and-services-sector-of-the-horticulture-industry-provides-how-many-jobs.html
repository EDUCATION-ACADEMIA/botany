The related products and services sector of the horticulture industry provides how many jobs?

(A) More than 100, 000

(B) More than 10 million

(C) More than 250, 000

(D) ** More than 750, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Horticulture contributes 30.4 per cent of the agriculture Gross Domestic Product (GDP) using only 13.1 per cent of gross cropped area.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Greenhouse Horticulture Market was estimated to be valued at $190.84 Million in 2021.

Concept note-3: -The horticulture sector in India produces around 320 million tons of products and contributes about 33% of the Gross Value Addition (GVA). It is a labour-intensive sector and thus provides a lot of employment opportunities, especially for the people of rural regions.
