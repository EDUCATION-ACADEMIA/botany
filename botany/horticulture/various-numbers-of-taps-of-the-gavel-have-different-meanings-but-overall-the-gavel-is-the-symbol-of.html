Various numbers of taps of the gavel have different meanings, but overall the gavel is the symbol of:

(A) ** authority

(B) FFA

(C) Voting

(D) weakness

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Various numbers of taps of the gavel have different meanings, but overall the gavel is the symbol of: Authority. Voice, rising, secret ballot and roll call are the four common methods of: Voting.

Concept note-2: -One tap of the gavel follows the announcement of adjournment, the completion of a business item or is a message to the members to be seated. Two taps of the gavel call the meeting to order. Three taps of the gavel is the signal for all members to stand in unison on the third tap.

Concept note-3: -To attract attention and call a meeting to order. In most organizations, two taps raise and one tap seats the assembly; in others, two taps raise and three taps seat it. To maintain order and restore it when breached in the course of the proceedings. (Tap the gavel once, but vigorously).

Concept note-4: -The first general purpose that some people have for giving speeches is to inform. Simply put, this is about helping audience members acquire information that they do not already possess.

Concept note-5: -Which fish system has the MOST influence on the type of feed used? Digestive.
