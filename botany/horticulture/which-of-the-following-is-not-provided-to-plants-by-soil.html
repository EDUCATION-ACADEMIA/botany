Which of the following is NOT provided to plants by soil?

(A) stability

(B) nutrients

(C) ** run-off

(D) oxygen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Soil contains minerals such as nitrogen, phosphorous and potassium that are essential for plants. Soil also holds water which is used by plants.

Concept note-2: -Plants provide us with food, fiber, shelter, medicine, and fuel. The basic food for all organisms is produced by green plants.

Concept note-3: -Answers. Water hyacinth is an aquatic plant, it does not need soil to grow.

Concept note-4: -Air, water, organic matter, and microorganism, all are components of soil, whereas texture is not a component of the soil, it is the characteristic feature of the soil based on which the soil is classified.
