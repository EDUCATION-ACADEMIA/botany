What percent is 5 of 7?

(A) 14 %

(B) ** 35 %

(C) 71 %

(D) 140 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We can see that this gives us the exact same answer as the first method: 7/35 as a percentage is 20%.

Concept note-2: -Solution: 5/35 as a percent is 14.286%

Concept note-3: -The total answers count 7-it’s 100%, so we to get a 1% value, divide 7 by 100 to get 0.07. Next, calculate the percentage of 5: divide 5 by 1% value (0.07), and you get 71.43%-it’s your percentage grade.

Concept note-4: -Solution: 7/30 as a percent is 23.333%
