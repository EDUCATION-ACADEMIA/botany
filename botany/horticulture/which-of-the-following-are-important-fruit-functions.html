Which of the following are important fruit functions?

(A) ** protection to seed

(B) food for embryo

(C) location of photosynthesis

(D) ** seed dispersal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dispersal of seeds is very important for the survival of plant species. If plants grow too closely together, they have to compete for light, water and nutrients from the soil. Seed dispersal allows plants to spread out from a wide area and avoid competing with one another for the same resources.

Concept note-2: -There are five main modes of seed dispersal: gravity, wind, ballistic, water, and by animals. Some plants are serotinous and only disperse their seeds in response to an environmental stimulus.

Concept note-3: -The most common methods are wind, water, animals, explosion and fire. Dandelion seeds float away in the wind.

Concept note-4: -A fruit protects the immature seeds from animals and extreme climatic conditions. It stores food material. It attracts animals that help in dispersing or scattering the seeds to distant places.
