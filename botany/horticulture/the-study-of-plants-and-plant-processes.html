The study of plants and plant processes

(A) Entomology

(B) Pomology

(C) Zeriscaping

(D) ** Botany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -botany, branch of biology that deals with the study of plants, including their structure, properties, and biochemical processes. Also included are plant classification and the study of plant diseases and of interactions with the environment.

Concept note-2: -A branch of science that deals with the structure, property, and classification of plants are Botany. The experts who study plants are called Botanists.

Concept note-3: -Plant physiology is the study of plant function and behaviour, encompassing all the dynamic processes of growth, metabolism, reproduction, defence, and communication that account for plants being alive (Salisbury & Ross, 1992; Baluška et al., 2006; Scott, 2008).

Concept note-4: -Herbology is the study of plants and plant lore. Botanists typically collect and identify different species of plant in order to make potions and remedies, and their compilations are known as ‘herbals’.
