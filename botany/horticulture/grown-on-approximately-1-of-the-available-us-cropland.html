Concept note-1: -Agricultural production is a major use of land, accounting for roughly 52 percent of the U.S. land base. (See data on Major Land Uses.)

Concept note-2: -The percentage of food crops in the total cropped area is 65%. Indian agriculture is dominated by food crops. They contribute about 50 percent of the total value of the output of agriculture. They are grown either as a single crop or in combination with other crops throughout the country.

Concept note-3: -Corn, soybeans, barley and oats The largest United States crop in terms of total production is corn, the majority of which is grown in a region known as the Corn Belt. The second largest crop grown in the United States is soybeans.
