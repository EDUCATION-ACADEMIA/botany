, the economic system of the United States, allows people to do business with a minimum of government interference

(A) Sole proprietorship

(B) Incorporation

(C) Capitalism

(D) ** Free enterprise

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Capitalism is the economic system in the United States. It is a market economy. Capitalism means that people, not the government, own most businesses.

Concept note-2: -The U.S. economy is a free enterprise system. That means that individuals-and not the government-own most of our countryâ€™s resources.

Concept note-3: -A market economy has freedom of choice and free enterprise. Private entrepreneurs are free to get and use resources and use them to produce goods and services. They are free to sell these goods and services in markets of their choice.

Concept note-4: -Capitalism is often thought of as an economic system in which private actors own and control property in accord with their interests, and demand and supply freely set prices in markets in a way that can serve the best interests of society.
