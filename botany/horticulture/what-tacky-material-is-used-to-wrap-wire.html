WHat tacky material is used to wrap wire

(A) floral foam

(B) ** floral tape

(C) flroal picks

(D) floral wire

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wax used. The wax released by a floral tape helps it stick on and hold the flowers in position. Make sure you buy a tape that only releases wax when stretched.

Concept note-2: -Floral Tape is made of crepe paper which is coated with sticky materials. It has strong elasticity, is an excellent choice for arrangements of fresh flowers, wired flowers and sugar flowers (fondant cake decorations), the colorful floral tape can blend well with the bouquets.

Concept note-3: -Floral Tape: There are a few types of floral tape. The most commonly used is called stem wrap. This tape is used to hold bouquets together as well as boutonnieres and corsages. The tape sticks to itself when pulled tight.
