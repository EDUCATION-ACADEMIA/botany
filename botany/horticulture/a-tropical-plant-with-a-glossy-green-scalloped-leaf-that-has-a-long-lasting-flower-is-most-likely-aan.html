A tropical plant with a glossy, green, scalloped leaf that has a long-lasting flower is most likely a/an:

(A) Christmas cactus.

(B) wax plant.

(C) emerald-ripple peperomia.

(D) ** kalanchoe.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aralia. The glossy green, scalloped leaves of aralia create a beautiful texture indoors.

Concept note-2: -A perennial that produces a long lasting colorful flower often used by florists. Japanese Boxwood.

Concept note-3: -A plant that has spikes with florets and is used to make floral arrangements is a/an: parlor palm. Which flowers have long stalks or spikes with florets? snapdragon.

Concept note-4: -Candytuft is a low-growing, spreading, evergreen ground cover that may be classified as herbaceous perennial or sub-shrub. It has a mounding and sprawling habit and measures 6 to 12 inches in height and 6 to 18 inches in width.
