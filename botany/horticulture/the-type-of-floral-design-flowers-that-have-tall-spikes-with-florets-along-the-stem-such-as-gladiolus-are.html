The type of floral design flowers that have tall spikes with florets along the stem such as gladiolus are:

(A) Filler

(B) Form

(C) ** Line

(D) Mass

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Line flower – A spike or spike-like inflorescence with an elongated stem, such as gladiolus, liatris, and larkspur.

Concept note-2: -Triangular flower arrangement – If you love unique things, you may like the beautiful triangular flower arrangement. Flowers are cut and trimmed in the form of a triangle. The tallest flower stem is used in the center, and other small are flowers are accordingly placed on the sides.

Concept note-3: -These building blocks encompass all flower varieties broken down into 4 categories: focal flowers, filler flowers, line flowers, and greenery. Each of the four types of flowers has a particular role to play and is an essential component in designing a balanced, lush, and visually appealing floral arrangement.

Concept note-4: -The concept of harmony helps to create a pleasing finished design. Each flower has a distinctive 3-dimensional form (shape). It is usually either a point, spherical, line, or transitional form. Flower forms all have a width, height and depth.

Concept note-5: -Aralia. The glossy green, scalloped leaves of aralia create a beautiful texture indoors.
