[SOURCES]
form flowers are used in floral design to:

(A) add mass

(B) cover frame

(C) ** create focal point

(D) establish outline

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The natural gradation of size encourages the eye to travel along the line of the spike. This element of line assists the designer in creating the focal point of the arrangement. Line flowers are also effective for establishing curvilinear lines in floral designs.

Concept note-2: -FOCAL POINT: The location within a design that attracts the most attention; the center of interest. In a corsage, the focal point is where the largest flower is placed.

Concept note-3: -Form Material Refers to flowers that have an outstanding shape that sets them apart. Orchids and exotic lilies are examples of form flowers.

Concept note-4: -The third element is form. Form includes three things height, width and depth. It is important to think about form while placing flowers because this is what determines the form. The fourth element is space, spacing of flowers give the viewer a chance to view each individual flower.
