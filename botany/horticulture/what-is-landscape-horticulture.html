What is landscape horticulture?

(A) ** The design, installation, and maintenance of landscapes

(B) The science of growing grass for sports play, lawns, and roadways

(C) Cultivation of vegetables and herbs for the home or market

(D) Fruit trees that are grown for display in a yard.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Landscaping is done with a view to create a natural scene by planting of lawn, trees and shrubs. It is the imitation of nature in the garden and improves the total living environment of the people.

Concept note-2: -Landscape design is an independent profession and a design and art tradition, practiced by landscape designers, combining nature and culture. In contemporary practice, landscape design bridges the space between landscape architecture and garden design.

Concept note-3: -The landscape design and management option will prepare you to design landscapes and manage landscape plants that provide cultural, ecological, and production functions. This option includes courses in plant identification and selection, design, construction, and installation.

Concept note-4: -The five steps of the design process include: 1) conducting a site inventory and analysis, 2) determining your needs, 3) creating functional diagrams, 4) developing conceptual design plans, and 5) drawing a final design plan.
