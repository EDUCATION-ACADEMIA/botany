When is it best to Harvest Peaches?

(A) ** May-September

(B) July Only

(C) May only

(D) August-March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -peach-picking.? May is the beginning of peach season, which continues until late September, but peach season is at its peak in July and August; no Fourth of July is complete without some fresh peach pie. Although the general peach season is in the summer, the growing location does affect the peak readiness of peaches.

Concept note-2: -Depending on the climate, peach season may occur anywhere between April and October. In general, peach season is during the summer, the ideal time for dining al fresco and feasting on fresh fruit.

Concept note-3: -It takes a peach about 4-5 months to mature and ripen. Peaches are harvested from the end of June until the end of August. Peaches come in three colors: White, yellow and red. Most peaches in the U.S. are yellow.

Concept note-4: -Peaches are at their peak from June to the end of August.
