Which of the following is NOT true for the benefits of plants/landscaping?

(A) acts as windbreakers

(B) provides a home for wildlife

(C) ** increases carbon dioxide levels

(D) increases property value

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the correct option is “D” (Photosynthesis).

Concept note-2: -Under elevated CO2 most plant species show higher rates of photosynthesis, increased growth, decreased water use and lowered tissue concentrations of nitrogen and protein. Rising CO2 over the next century is likely to affect both agricultural production and food quality.

Concept note-3: -Carbon dioxide in the atmosphere warms the planet, causing climate change. Human activities have raised the atmosphere’s carbon dioxide content by 50% in less than 200 years.

Concept note-4: -Higher concentrations of carbon dioxide make plants more productive because photosynthesis relies on using the sun’s energy to synthesise sugar out of carbon dioxide and water. Plants and ecosystems use the sugar both as an energy source and as the basic building block for growth.
