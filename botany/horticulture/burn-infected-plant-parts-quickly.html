BOTANY

HORTICULTURE

[SOURCES]
Burn infected plant parts quickly.

(A) FALSE

(B) ** TRUE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cut off any infected leaves. Then spray the leaves with a mixture of milk and water (around 17 ounces of milk and one gallon of water) each week. The leaves and stem wilt, turn yellow, and dry out.

Concept note-2: -Typical symptoms include yellow leaves dried from the edges, smaller plants which can also be sterile, and malformed or rotten cobs.

Concept note-3: -Some viruses can infect plants when aphids and other insects tap into the phloem to feed. Such insect vectors can also pick up virus particles and carry them to new plant hosts. Other viruses infect plant cells through a wound site created by a leaf-munching insect such as a beetle.

Concept note-4: -Plants respond to infection using a two-branched innate immune system. The first branch recognizes and responds to molecules common to many classes of microbes, including non-pathogens. The second responds to pathogen virulence factors, either directly or through their effects on host targets.
