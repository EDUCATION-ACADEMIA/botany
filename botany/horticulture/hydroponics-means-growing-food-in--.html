Hydroponics means growing food in ____ ?

(A) ** Water

(B) Specially formulated soil

(C) Special chemicals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hydroponics is a type of horticulture and a subset of hydroculture which involves growing plants, usually crops or medicinal plants, without soil, by using water-based mineral nutrient solutions in aqueous solvents.

Concept note-2: -: the growing of plants in nutrient solutions with or without an inert medium (such as soil) to provide mechanical support.

Concept note-3: -The technique of growing plants without soil is called hydroponics. It’s simply growing plants in water.

Concept note-4: -What is hydroponics? Hydroponics comes from the Greek word “hydro, ” meaning water, and “ponos, ” meaning labor. In other words, hydroponics is gardening without soil. Growing food in a desert can be difficult because of extreme temperatures, low natural precipitation and limited arable soil.

Concept note-5: -The technique of cultivating plants in a nutrient solution without the use of soil is known as Hydroponics.
