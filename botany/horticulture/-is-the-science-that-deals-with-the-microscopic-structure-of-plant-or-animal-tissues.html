____ is the science that deals with the microscopic structure of plant or animal tissues.

(A) ** Histology

(B) Morphology

(C) Cytology

(D) Hypocotyl

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Histology, also known as microscopic anatomy or microanatomy, is the branch of biology that studies the microscopic anatomy of biological tissues.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is A histology. The study of tissues is called histology. Cytology is the study of cells. Embryology is the study of the development of an embryo. Morphology is the study of the external structural features of an organism.

Concept note-3: -The study of tissues and cells under a microscope.

Concept note-4: -The part of science dealing with plant and animal tissues is known as histology. It is a mostly descriptive discipline that uses light and electron microscopy to describe tissue morphology.

Concept note-5: -Tissues are composed of similar types of cells that work in a coordinated fashion to perform a common task, and the study of the tissue level of biological organization is histology.
