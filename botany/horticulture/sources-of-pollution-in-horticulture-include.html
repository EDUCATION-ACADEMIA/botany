Sources of pollution in horticulture include

(A) ** Refuelling and using powered equipment

(B) ** Storing and using herbicides

(C) ** Transporting fuel between sites

(D) ** Producing and disposing of waste

(E) ** Composting green waste

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Horticulture waste means wastes generated in the orchards, parks and gardens and roadside plants, trees, bushes, creepers and any green foliage.

Concept note-2: -If a compost pile is anaerobic, there are more serious consequences than the stench. When a pile doesnâ€™t have oxygen, it emits methane, a harmful greenhouse gas, according to the United States Composting Council. This is one of the reasons why landfills harm the environment.
