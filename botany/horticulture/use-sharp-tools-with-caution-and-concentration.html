Use sharp tools with caution and concentration.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: We should always use sharp tools WITH CAUTION to avoid danger or something.

Concept note-2: -Always use sharp tools with caution and concentration to avoid cuts to hands and fingers.

Concept note-3: -Sharp tools produce better work, cut with less effort and can be safer to use because you do not have to force them, so there is less pressure behind the cutting edge.

Concept note-4: -A sharp point or edge is very thin and can cut through things very easily. A sharp knife, tool, or other object has a point or edge of this kind.

Concept note-5: -Keep your pruners and other cutting tools sharp with a specialized pruner-sharpening tool or a sharpening stone. Other tools such as hoes, shovels, and knives can be kept sharp with quick touchups from a sharpening file, followed by a sharpening stone.
