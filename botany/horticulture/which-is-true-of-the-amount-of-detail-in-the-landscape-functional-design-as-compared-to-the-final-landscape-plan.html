Which is true of the amount of detail in the landscape functional design as compared to the final landscape plan?

(A) ** less

(B) more

(C) one-half

(D) same

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Functional landscape means landscape at a park, school, public recreation area or privately-operated common area that is utilized for recreational/leisure activities by more than a single residence.

Concept note-2: -Balance in Landscape Design Balance is the concept of evenly distributing weight throughout the plan. Balance can be achieved with symmetry or asymmetry. Symmetrical balance (or formal balance) occurs when two sides of the landscape are identical mirror images of each other.

Concept note-3: -The principles of landscape design include the elements of unity, scale, balance, simplicity, variety, emphasis, and sequence as they apply to line, form, texture, and color. These elements are interconnected.
