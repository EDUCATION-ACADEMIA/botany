Which of the following careers is responsible for the installation of landscapes?

(A) Landscape architects

(B) ** Landscape contracting

(C) Landscape maintenance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Landscapers create and maintain gardens, parks and other outdoor areas.

Concept note-2: -Landscape architects design attractive and functional public parks, gardens, playgrounds, residential areas, college campuses, and public spaces. They also plan the locations of buildings, roads, walkways, flowers, shrubs, and trees within these environments.

Concept note-3: -Plants new flowers, trees, grass, hedges, and bushes. Maintains gardens and lawns, including mowing, trimming, pruning, raking, and weeding. Waters plants and lawns and adds fertilizer as needed. Installs outdoor lighting and sprinkler systems.
