What is a potential source of danger called?

(A) Accident

(B) ** Hazard

(C) Right-to-know

(D) Materials safety data sheet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A hazard is any source of potential damage, harm or adverse health effects on something or someone. Basically, a hazard is the potential for harm or an adverse effect (for example, to people as health effects, to organizations as property or equipment losses, or to the environment).

Concept note-2: -A hazard is a source or a situation with the potential for harm in terms of human injury or ill-health, damage to property, damage to the environment, or a combination of these.

Concept note-3: -Potential Hazard means a danger to health or safety which may occur if corrective action is not taken.

Concept note-4: -16/06/2021 | Document. A hazard is a dangerous phenomenon, substance, human activity or condition. It may cause loss of life, injury or other health impacts, property damage, loss of livelihoods and services, social and economic disruption, or environmental damage.

Concept note-5: -The pond is a potential hazard for children, but it does have a removable cover. Mobile phones have been regarded as potential health hazards almost since they were invented. Before new toys are sold, potential hazards will need to be identified through a safety assessment.
