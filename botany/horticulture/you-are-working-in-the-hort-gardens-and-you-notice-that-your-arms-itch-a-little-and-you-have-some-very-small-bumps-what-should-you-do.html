You are working in the hort gardens and you notice that your arms itch a little and you have some very small bumps. What should you do?

(A) Run around screaming in panic about how Ms. Ventle gave you poison ivy!

(B) Stop work immediately, gossip about the bumps for 10 minutes then excuse yourself to the nurse without asking for permission.

(C) ** Acknowledge that sometimes life itches, then calmly wash the area with cool water and pat dry.

(D) Ignore it, then go home and tell your parents that you were made to suffer through a life threatening allergy.
