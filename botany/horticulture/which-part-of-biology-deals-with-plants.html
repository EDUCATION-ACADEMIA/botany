Which part of biology deals with plants?

(A) oncologists

(B) gardener

(C) ** Botany

(D) horticulture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -botany, branch of biology that deals with the study of plants, including their structure, properties, and biochemical processes.

Concept note-2: -Botany is the scientific study of plants-how plants function, what they look like, how they are related to each other, where they grow, how people make use of plants, and how plants evolved.

Concept note-3: -Botany: Botany is the branch of science that deals with the morphology, anatomy, structure, functions, and all biochemical processes of plants.

Concept note-4: -The branch of science which deals with the study of different types of plants is known as botany. The investigation of the inner parts, their association, and function in the plants is known as plant life structures. In other words, which is normally utilized for this investigation is phytotomy.
