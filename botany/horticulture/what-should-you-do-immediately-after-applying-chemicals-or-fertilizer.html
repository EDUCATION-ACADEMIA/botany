What should you do immediately after applying chemicals or fertilizer?

(A) ** Wash your hands

(B) Eat Lunch

(C) Water your plants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Proper cleaning procedures should always be followed after applying pesticides. To remove pesticide residues, use a bucket to thoroughly rinse tools or equipment that you used when mixing the pesticide. Pour the rinse water into the pesticide sprayer and dispose of it by applying to a labeled site.

Concept note-3: -Always wear eye protection, gloves, long-sleeve shirt and long-leg pants when handling chemicals, and take your time when mixing. Triple-rinse empty pesticide containers and do not reuse them; dispose of empty containers properly after rinsing.
