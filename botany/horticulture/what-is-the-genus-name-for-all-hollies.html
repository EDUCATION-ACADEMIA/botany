What is the genus name for all hollies?

(A) Quercus

(B) Acer

(C) Cornus

(D) ** Ilex

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -holly, (genus Ilex), genus of some 600 species of shrubs and trees in the family Aquifoliaceae, distributed nearly worldwide. Several species are cultivated as ornamentals for their distinctive foliage and red or black fruits, which persist into winter and are popular Christmas decorations.

Concept note-2: -Ilex aquifolium, commonly called English holly, is native to Europe, western Asia and north Africa.

Concept note-3: -Holly or Ilex, is a genus made of 400-600 species including both evergreen and deciduous trees, shrubs and climbing plants.

Concept note-4: -Ilex aquifolium, the holly, common holly, English holly, European holly, or occasionally Christmas holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae, native to western and southern Europe, northwest Africa, and southwest Asia.
