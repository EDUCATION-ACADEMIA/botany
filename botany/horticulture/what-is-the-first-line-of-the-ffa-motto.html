What is the first line of the FFA Motto?

(A) Living to Serve

(B) Earning to Live

(C) ** Learning to Do

(D) Doing to Learn

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Learning to Do, Doing to Learn, Earning to Live, Living to Serve.

Concept note-2: -“Doing to Learn” signifies that individuals learn most when they do things them-selves. “Earning to Live, ” gives the under-standing that the organization provides great value in learning about career oppor-tunities through supervised agricultural experience programs.

Concept note-3: -Our members live the motto Learning to Do, Doing to Learn, Earning to Live and Living to Serve. FFA members rise to the challenge of service embracing members of all walks of life united through FFA.

Concept note-4: -The first paragraph means that in the future we will always have farms and it will never go away. but to do this we have to teach kids how to farm and how important farming is.

Concept note-5: -Turn the Creed into a rap to help practice your pauses and sentence tempo. Create a dance that helps you remember your footwork and choreography when presenting on stage. If you are musically inclined, grab an instrument and turn the Creed into a song. Memorize words through different mediums.
